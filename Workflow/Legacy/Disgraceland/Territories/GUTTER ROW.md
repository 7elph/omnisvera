# GUTTER ROW


<h2>Overview</h2>

> [!NOTE|clean no-i right]+ ‎GUTTER ROW
> ![[gutt1.png|400]]  

Gutter Row is the choking heart of Disgraceland — a lawless sprawl of rusted rooftops, splintered alleyways, and desperate humanity. It's where the black market thrives, the weak vanish, and the rules don’t apply. Smoke curls from every rooftop. The streets reek of sweat, piss, rot, and fried rat. You don’t come here unless you have to — and if you do, you don’t linger.

By far the most overpopulated zone in all of United Tribucia, Gutter Row stretches endlessly in every direction, packed shoulder-to-shoulder with shanties, scrap-built towers, and narrow corridors crawling with barterers, thieves, and worse. The economy runs on favors, smuggling, and the unspoken understanding that everything — and everyone — has a price. The slave trade operates in plain sight, tucked just behind the street food vendors and stolen tech booths.

Locals carve their lives out in the districts of [[GUTTER ROW#Vileby|Vileby]], [[GUTTER ROW#Nailstooth|Nailstooth]], [[GUTTER ROW#Pisstown|Pisstown]], [[GUTTER ROW#The Docks|The Docks]], [[GUTTER ROW#Reapton|Reapton]], Garbage Bay, and [[GUTTER ROW#Crossyork|Crossyork]] — each one worse than the last, each with its own twisted rhythm. There's no safety, only familiarity. People grow up fast here. Or they don’t grow up at all.

The so-called police force is a unit known as [[Loyalist Guard#Blackforce|Blackforce]], though most call them _Black Shirts_. Dressed in matte black armor with emotionless masks and reinforced batons, they patrol sporadically — more interested in keeping tribute flowing to [[The Duke]] than protecting anyone. When they do show up, it’s usually to silence someone who said too much.

Gutter Row isn’t just where the forgotten live. It’s where power hides. Where secrets are bought in whispers. And where a match, dropped in the right place, could burn the whole rotten thing down.

<h2>Districts</h2>

#### Vileby

The black heart of Gutter Row. **Vileby** is where the Three Nail Market churns night and day, pumping illicit deals into every vein of Tribucia. [[Third Row]] gang runs things from the shadows — and sometimes not-so-shadowy rooftops. Vileby Hills rises just north, where the [[Murray Boys]] hold court in their fortified mansion. It’s the only place in Gutter Row where guards wear suits, and deals end with champagne instead of switchblades.

---

#### Nailstooth

A place even Gutter Row winces at. **Nailstooth** is where you land when you’ve got nothing left — not even the decency to die in a proper alley. It’s all rusted iron, tar-paper roofs, and barking dogs with ribs showing. The ground smells like rot. The people don't flinch when guns go off. You don’t walk into Nailstooth. You get dragged there.

---

#### The Docks

Southernmost point in all of Tribucia, **The Docks** is a salt-bitten maze of boats, rusted cranes, and rat-infested boardwalks. Fishermen haul in whatever the Dead Waters will spare, and families scrape by in tide-worn homes. When the fog rolls in, it feels like the world ends here — and maybe it should.

---

#### Crossyork

If Gutter Row has a postcard, it's **Crossyork** — and even that’s water-stained and half-torched. The mayor lives here behind iron bars and blackout curtains, trying to pretend the rest of the Row doesn’t exist. But the Rustwater Raiders haven’t forgotten. They hit often, sailing in from the Four Tombs Islands just off the coast. Crossyork smells like brine, smoke, and fresh betrayal.

---

#### Pisstown

No place bleeds like **Pisstown**. It’s where dreams die screaming and legends are born twisted. [[Dusty Dillon]] grew up singing in the alleys here. [[Ora Razchek]] built his death house above it all. And somewhere in the fog, [[The Grim Man]] stalks like a goddamn ghost with a grudge. Pisstown’s streets eat souls and spit bones. If you’re here, you’ve already lost something.

---

#### Reapton

They call it the working spine of Gutter Row — bent, cracked, and somehow still upright. **Reapton** is where the [[Tribucia Tribute]] gets printed, and where proud folks grind out survival without a safety net. It's paperboys dodging knives. It's ink-stained hands and busted typewriters. If Gutter Row has a conscience, it’s buried somewhere beneath Reapton’s mud.

---

#### Savage Hill

Everything’s louder in **Savage Hill**. The wind off Garbage Coast blows sour through these streets, but laughter still rises above it — the kind that hides pain. The **Ha Ha Hole** comedy club lights up the block, defiant and alive. Outside, trash fires burn, rats riot, and broken TVs scream static. But inside, jokes land harder than fists.

---

#### South Point

Close to Ashmoor, far from the madness. **South Point** is a sleepy stretch where a few local farmers till what land they can. The soil’s no good, the water’s worse, but the air isn’t poison and the nights are quiet. Folks here keep their heads down and their fences mended. It ain’t paradise — but it ain’t Pisstown either.

---

#### Red Light District

Vice without apology. **The Red Light District** is Gutter Row's fever dream, lit by flickering neon and fueled by broken morals. The **Hollow House**, run by [[Shana]], is the beating heart — a brothel, a fortress, a sanctuary of sin. Luxyn dealers, synth clubs, fight dens. You want it, it’s here. Just remember, payment comes due — one way or another.
