# Luxyn ("White Delight")

> [!NOTE|clean no-i right]+ LUXYN PROPAGANDA POSTER
> ![[luxyn.png|500]]  

## Overview
**Type:** Synthetic euphoric drug  
**Street Names:** White Delight, Blank, Ghostrocks, Snowdrop, Glow  
**Produced in:** [[Charspire]] (from mined elements in [[Gutter Row]])  
**Main Markets:** [[Gutter Row]], [[Charspire]]  
**Overseen by:** Mayor [[Gorren Brile]] & Mayor [[Cricket Granger]] (secretly)  
**Distributed by:** [[Murray Boys]]


---

## Description

**Luxyn** is a white, faintly glowing powder—almost looks illuminated in the dark. The main ingredient is extracted from deep mines in Gutter Row, then shipped to Charspire, where it’s refined and cut for the streets.

Most users snort Luxyn for the fastest hit. Desperate addicts will eat it straight, chasing even a hint of euphoria. It has a faintly sweet, chemical taste, and leaves a cold, tingling numbness in the mouth and throat.

---

## Effects

**Upsides:**
- Instant, powerful euphoria: All pain, fear, hunger, sadness, and regret vanish.
- The world feels “soft and beautiful.”  
- Lasts 3–6 hours.

**Downsides:**
- Highly addictive. Chasing the first high becomes all-consuming.
- Overdose is common, especially with street-cut Luxyn. Signs: violent shaking, vomiting, then cardiac arrest.
- **“White Eyes”:** Prolonged or heavy use turns the user’s eyes milk-white and eventually blinds them. Addicts with this look are called **Blanks**.
- Withdrawal: Debilitating pain, hallucinations, severe depression. Some die from the process.

---

## Culture & Trade

- **Main Hubs:** Gutter Row (mining, distribution), Charspire (processing, cutting, packaging).
- **Power Structure:** Overseen by mayors Brile (Charspire) and Granger (Gutter Row) in a secret alliance. The two split profits and keep supply flowing, all while avoiding Loyalist Guard raids and the Duke’s notice.
- **Street Dealers:** Sell openly in alleys, clubs, and “pain clinics.” The [NAME MAFIA GROUP] enforces turf and quality.
- **Duke’s Stance:** The Duke is strictly anti-drug. If he discovers Brile and Granger’s involvement, both are dead men walking.

---

## Slang & Lore

- **White Delight:** Most popular street name.
- **Blanks:** Heavy users with fully white, blind eyes.
- **Glow:** The faint shine of pure Lumin.
- **“Going Blank”:** Losing yourself to addiction.
- **“Taste the Light”:** Dealer slang for dosing up.

---

## Visuals

- Luxyn is packaged in small waxed paper envelopes, each stamped with a crude symbol (different for each dealer or mafia crew).
- Blanks are a common sight in Gutter Row and Charspire—shuffling, blind, lost in their heads.
