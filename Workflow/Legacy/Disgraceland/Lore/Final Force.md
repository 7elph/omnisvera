# FINAL FORCE a.k.a The F.F

> [!column] Information
>> [!rustwater] Description
> **Type:** Elite Special Forces Military Unit, Loyalist Guard Special Division
> **Nicknames:** FF, The Iron Dogs, [[The Duke]]’s Hammers
> **Commander:** [[Sgt. Rottwyler]]
> **Headquarters:** Loyalist Base [[Ashmoor]], with mobile deployment units stationed in armories across Disgraceland.
> 
>> [!rustwater] Final Force
>> ![[ff.png|400]]


## Uniform & Gear  
- Armored “Iron” Suits—bulky, steel-gray, inspired by early Iron Man Model-1 design.
- Features:  
    - Heavy, riveted plating  
    - Grated faceplates (slitted eyes, grated mouth)  
    - Short-burst flight (rocket-leap or glide)  
    - Built-in arm cannons: concussive blasts, incendiaries, slug launchers  
    - Shoulder/back mini-missile racks (some units)  
    - Power: experimental fusion cells or chemical batteries (dangerous, unreliable)
- **[[Sgt. Rottwyler]]s suit:** Larger, custom-forged, extra weapons, scarred plating, dog tags welded into the chest.

## Selection  
- Only the toughest, most loyal, or most feared [[Loyalist Guard]]s get “volunteered.”
- Suits are bio-locked to each trooper.

## Reputation  
- Feared by civilians and rebels; even Loyalist Guards keep their distance.
- The FF is deployed only for major threats or to “make an example.”
- Their arrival is announced by the roar of jets and the thunder of armored boots.
- Rumors: Some suits malfunction and cook the pilot alive.  
- Some believe the suits can run on “ghost mode,” piloting themselves after the wearer dies.

## Tactics  
- Shock & Awe: Enter loud, crush resistance fast.
- No mercy. Rottwyler kills his own for cowardice or failure.
- Heavy support by engineers/mechanics; suits require constant upkeep.

## Infamous Event: The Blackshift Massacre

> “No one forgets the day the Iron Dogs marched into the mines. The smoke still hangs over [[Charspire]].”

In 2178, the Duke deployed the Final Force to [[Charspire]] after miners at the Blackshift Pit staged a mass walkout over starvation rations and lethal conditions. What started as a tense standoff turned to horror when the FF, led by [[Sgt. Rottwyler]], breached the mine gates and unleashed their arsenal. Now known as the [[Blackshift Massacre]].

The massacre was swift and total. Workers—men, women, even children hiding in supply rooms—were slaughtered without warning.  
The event might have faded into rumor, but a reporter from the **[[Tribucia Tribute]]** was present, snapping photos from a hidden alcove as the carnage unfolded. Those photos, smuggled out and copied in secret, became symbols of resistance across Disgraceland.

To this day, “Blackshift” is a curse in Charspire. It’s a name whispered when the Duke’s iron hand comes down. Even Loyalist Guards remember it as a black scar on the regime—a day the world saw the true face of the Final Force.
