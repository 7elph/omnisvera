# 🏴 [[Poundball]]

> [!NOTE|clean no-i right]+ POUNDBALL
> ![[pound.png|450]]  

**Poundball** is a brutal, fast-paced sport played throughout Disgraceland in abandoned lots, broken parking decks, and dusty fields. It’s a game of speed, force, and survival — where glory is earned through blood and bruises. Banned in [[Ashmoor]] but beloved everywhere else, Poundball is more than a game — it’s a warzone with rules.

---

## 🧱 Basic Rules

- **Teams of 6:**
  - **3 Runners** – Only players allowed to carry or pass the Poundball.
  - **2 Pounders** – Defense. Smash and stop opposing runners by any means.
  - **1 Anchor** – A grounded goalie. Cannot leave the goal zone.

- **The Ball:**
  A 25-pound leather-wrapped sand-and-scrap core called the *Poundball*.

- **Field:**
  Played on any wide-open space. Each end has a marked **scoring zone** — sometimes spray-painted, sometimes built from junk.

- **Scoring:**
  A runner scores by:
  - Crossing into the scoring zone while holding the ball (3 points), or
  - Underhand-lobbing it into the "zone", usually a trashcan, from outside (1 point).

- **Turnovers:**
  If a runner is tackled and drops the ball before scoring or passing, possession flips to the other team after three hits if the Runner still holds the ball.

- **Passing:**
  Lateral or underhand only. No overhand forward throws.

- **Winning:**
  First team to 13 points or the most points in 45 minutes.

---

## 🚫 Fouls & Penalties

- **Illegal moves:** Eye-gouging, biting, groin shots.
- **Grifter Drops:** Faking a drop to bait a hit. Results in a **Grime Stop**.
- **Scrap Box:** Players penalized for dirty play serve 3 minutes in a caged side zone.

## 🥊 Positions & Strategy

| Role       | Description                        |
|------------|------------------------------------|
| **Runner** | Agile, strategic, good hands       |
| **Pounder**| Massive, mean, relentless hitters  |
| **Anchor** | Stationary juggernaut, goal guard  |

**Tactics:**
- **"Bleeding the Field"** – Luring defenders to one side for a clean run.
- **"Gravel Grind"** – A low crawl burst to sneak past defenders.
- **"Fleshing the Anchor"** – Forcing the goalie to commit so another runner can score.

---

## 🎭 Poundball Attire

Players wear scavenged, makeshift gear for protection and intimidation:

- **Runners:**
  - Stripped-down outfits for speed.
  - Gloves, elbow wraps, light pads.
  - Often shirtless or in cut tanks.

- **Pounders:**
  - **Makeshift shoulder pads** fashioned from metal scraps, car seat foam, or layered rubber.
  - Reinforced boots, padded forearms, chain wraps.

- **Anchors:**
  - Heavy torso padding.
  - **Leather helmets**, some with horn-like additions or personal paintjobs.
  - Thick gloves for bone-breaking tackles.

Many paint their faces or bodies with ash, rust, or blood before matches. Team symbols are often carved or stitched into their gear.

## 🏆 Poundball Culture

Poundball is more than entertainment — it’s a release. A way for citizens to forget about Loyalists, rations, or chains. Betting is rampant. Fans chant in the stands made of shipping crates, and riots often break out when a ref call goes wrong.

Kids play it with bricks and trashballs in alleys. Adults die playing it for glory. Legends are born in the dust and blood of the Poundball pit.
