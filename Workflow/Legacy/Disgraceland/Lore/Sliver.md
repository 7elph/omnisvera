#  Sliver

> "Luck’s a lie. In *Sliver*, you either *bleed for your win* or you lose with style." — [[Ninepack]]

## 🩸 Overview

> [!NOTE|clean no-i right]+ game of sliver
> ![[sliver.png|450]]

**Sliver** is a rogue-deckbuilder card game born in the underbelly of [[Gutter Row]] and [[Steeltown]]. Inspired by old-world poker but twisted by post-collapse desperation, it's part strategy, part madness.

Some say the game was first played with bones and blood in the [[Three Nail Market]]

---

## 🕹 How to Play

### 🎯 Objective
Build hands to score **Influence** each round. First to **100 Influence** wins. In outlaw variants, the winner takes all—coin, secrets, or lives.

### 👥 Players
2–4 players (standard); up to 6 in high-stakes variants.

### 🃏 Deck Composition

#### Standard Deck (64 cards):
- Suits:
  - **Bones** (Spades)
  - **Ashes** (Clubs)
  - **Blood** (Hearts)
  - **Coin** (Diamonds)
- Ranks: 1–10 per suit
- 4 "Wild" Sliver-backed cards (draw from **Sliver Deck**)

#### Sliver Deck (20 cards):
Unique power cards that warp reality and strategy. Drawn through combos or faction effects.

#### Modifier Deck (30 cards):
Cards that grant permanent or round-limited buffs/debuffs. Includes:
- **Scar Cards** (persistent effects)
- **Rust Cards** (negative mutations)
- **Favor Cards** (faction or character boosts)
- **Dust Cards** (instant plays, volatile)

---

## 🔄 Game Loop

1. **Draw Phase**
   - Each player draws **7 base cards**.
   - If a card or effect allows, draw from the **Sliver Deck** or **Modifier Deck**.

2. **Build Hands**
   - Players arrange their 7 cards into a single **poker-style hand**.
   - Traditional hands apply (Pair, Three-of-a-Kind, Straight, Flush, etc.).
   - Some Sliver or Modifier cards allow players to split into **multiple hands**.

3. **Play Phase**
   - Play up to 2 Sliver or Modifier cards before revealing.
   - Cards may enhance hand strength, add multipliers, or sabotage others.

4. **Reveal Phase**
   - Players reveal hands simultaneously.
   - Hands are scored based on tier + any effects.
   - Highest hand wins the **Influence Pool** for the round.
   - Second-highest earns **1 Influence**, others get nothing.

5. **Burn Phase**
   - All played cards go to discard except Scar and Rust (they persist).
   - Draw back to 7. Repeat.

## 🧠 Hand Scoring Example

| Hand Type        | Influence Gain | Notes                                |
|------------------|----------------|--------------------------------------|
| Pair             | +2             | Basic combo                          |
| Two Pair         | +4             | Common early-game score              |
| Three-of-a-Kind  | +6             | Moderate score, combo friendly       |
| Straight         | +8             | Sequential, hard to block            |
| Flush            | +10            | Same suit                            |
| Full House       | +13            | Rare, strong                         |
| Four-of-a-Kind   | +16            | Unlocks special Sliver draw          |
| Straight Flush   | +20            | May trigger **Vision** effects       |
| Bone Royal (10-9-8-7-6, Bones) | +30 | Legendary, rare as sin              |

## 💀 Sliver Cards (Drawn Separately)

| Name                        | Effect                                                                 |
|-----------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **[[The Grim Man]]**        | Destroy lowest scoring card on all other players' hands                |
| **[[Dusty Dillon]]'s Echo** | If you make a Straight, score 1.5x and draw one Modifier next round    |
| **Gable’s Signal**          | Cancel one opponent’s Modifier this round                             |
| **Widow’s Mercy**           | If you lose this round, deal 3 damage to a chosen player’s deck        |
| **Steeltown Switch**        | Swap hands (at random) with one player before reveal                   |
| **Double Duke**             | Your hand score is doubled, but you draw 1 less card next round        |

## ☠ Modifier Cards

### Scar Cards (Persistent)

| Name           | Effect                                                            |
|----------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Burnt Nerve    | Lowest scoring hand is doubled each round                        |
| Hollow Crown   | Start each game with one Sliver draw                             |
| Bone Tattoo    | Flushes of Bones grant +4 extra Influence                        |

### Rust Cards (Negative)

| Name         | Effect                                                      |
|--------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|
| Brittle Deck | Max hand size is reduced by 1                              |
| Flickering Eye | You must reveal your hand early each round               |
| Cracked Memory | You cannot use the same Modifier more than once per game |

## 🔥 Culture & Setting

- **Backburn rules** apply in underground games. All modifiers allowed. Real-world stakes often replace Influence.
- Winners may demand weapons, favors, secrets, or blood.
- Famous tables:
  - **The Red Table** in [[GUTTER ROW#Vileby|Vileby]]
  - **The Runglow Cart** (a mobile Sliver game hidden inside a food vendor)
  - **Dust Alley Den** behind the [[Rustwater Raiders]]' tavern
