## ☣️ Design & Composition

The **Tetra Bomb** was the result of a last-ditch black project from the Unified Continental Defense Pact during the height of the **[[Water Wars]]** — a conflict already choking the planet’s final resources.

The bomb’s name refers to its four core components:

1. **Helionite Plasma** – A synthetic isotope that destabilized upon impact with moisture  
2. **Black Crysilin Dust** – A nano-powder that fed on electrical currents and atmospheric pressure  
3. **Red Virex** – A biochemical ignition gel that reacted violently with oxygenated blood  
4. **Human Nervous Tissue** – Taken from dying test subjects. No explanation ever declassified.

The fourth element was added late. It worked.

---

## 💀 Deployment

The bomb was **not tested**. No simulation was conclusive. The scientists and strategists that signed off on its use assumed a localized continental sterilization effect — a final scare tactic.

What happened instead was **a cascade of chain reactions** across the stratosphere. Weather patterns collapsed. Power grids melted. Any nearby biome — human, animal, or machine — stopped functioning.

And then, silence.

---

## 🌎 Aftermath

Surprisingly… nature survived.

| Element             | Status After Detonation |
|---------------------|-------------------------|
| **Fresh Water**     | Largely unaffected      |
| **Plant Life**      | Regrew within months    |
| **Human Civilization** | Functionally erased |
| **Digital Infrastructure** | Obliterated |
| **Nuclear Fallout** | None – Tetra had no uranium, no fusion ring. |

The only immediate result:  
**One long, endless winter.**  
Cold enough to kill the desperate. Quiet enough to bury the loud.  
A thousand unmarked graves frozen in the open.

---

## 🧬 Legacy

- Tetra was only detonated once.  
- The schematics were **never recovered**.  
- The name “Tetra” is now used in **curses, prayers, and warnings**.  
- Some say remnants of the fourth element — the **nervous tissue core** — are still out there, buried beneath Dead Waters.  
- Others claim the **Reckoning Coil** is its second birth.
