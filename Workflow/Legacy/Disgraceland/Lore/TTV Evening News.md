# TTV Evening News

> [!NOTE|clean no-i right]+ ‎TTV EVENING NEWS
> ![[en.png|400]]  

> “United we stand, informed we thrive.”  
> — Warren Scottyard, opening line to every broadcast

## Overview  
The **TTV Evening News** is the primary nightly news broadcast aired across all of United Tribucia, streaming directly into every home via the mandatory CRT television sets issued by The Duke. Airing five nights a week at **18:00 sharp**, the program recaps daily events across the six major regions, delivers weather updates, and highlights notable achievements of Loyalist programs.

While the show claims impartiality, it's heavily censored and curated by the **Tribucian Communication Bureau**, ensuring all content either supports Loyalist rule or tactfully avoids criticizing it. Citizens see the show as either a dependable news source or subtle propaganda, depending on who you ask — and where you live.

The broadcast originates from **Studio 3B at TTV Headquarters in Gravenwild, Charspire**, the same complex where [[Night Time with Morris Gable]] is filmed.

## 🧑‍💼 Anchors

### [[Warren Scottyard]]
**Role:** Lead Anchor  
**Background:** Once a decorated field journalist during the reconstruction years, [[Warren Scottyard]] earned his reputation covering The Duke’s rise in Ashmoor. With a deep, authoritative voice and icy composure, Warren is both respected and distrusted depending on who's watching. He's seen as the "face of facts" by Loyalists and the "voice of polished lies" by skeptics.  
**Style:** Calm, unwavering, textbook Loyalist professional. Tends to pause dramatically before stating statistics or issuing official statements.

> “The Duke reminds us tonight — stability is not free. It is earned.”

---

### [[Layla Hawk]]
**Role:** Co-Anchor & Field Correspondent  
**Background:** A younger, sharper contrast to Warren, [[Layla Hawk]] was handpicked from a pool of regional broadcasters in Threshton for her charisma and on-camera presence. Rumors suggest she’s the niece of a former Loyalist commander, but nothing's confirmed. Layla covers softer segments, like weather, daily spotlights, and public health alerts, but occasionally handles field reports in more dangerous regions like Gutter Row or Steeltown.  
**Style:** Relatable, conversational, often smirking beneath the surface. A few viewers suspect she’s not quite as Loyalist-aligned as she lets on.

> “Sun’s rising early in Threshton tomorrow — but not as early as the ranchers hope. Grain quotas remain unmet for the fourth week running.”

---

## 🎥 Segments

- **The State Rundown:** Quick summaries from each region. Loyalist-approved only.  
- **Weather from the Watchtower:** Regional weather provided via airship telemetry and ground scouts.  
- **Public Safety Alerts:** Official bulletins from the Guard Forces. Often vague or cryptic.  
- **Faces of Tribucia:** Human-interest pieces focused on "model citizens" and Loyalist success stories.  
- **Field Reports:** Pre-recorded dispatches from reporters embedded across the regions.

---

## 🕯️ Cultural Impact

The TTV Evening News remains required viewing for many Loyalist families and a tolerated background noise in poorer households where it doubles as a light source. In black markets and backrooms, recordings are often edited into parody broadcasts known as "False Flags" — spliced with unflattering cuts, subversive commentary, or ghosted transmissions that include forbidden footage.

Layla Hawk has a secret following in Steeltown zines, where her smirks and unscripted side comments are interpreted as signs of quiet resistance.
