## 🕰 Overview
*Night Time with Morris Gable* is the flagship late-night broadcast of [[TTV Studios]], aimed at soothing the nerves of a fractured nation while subtly reinforcing loyalty to the regime. Airing five nights a week across all of [[DISGRACELAND]], it delivers charm, distraction, and just enough optimism to keep people passive.

The show is not mandatory, but it might as well be. CRT sets gifted by [[The Duke]] ensure everyone has access. In many homes, Morris is the only voice still speaking when the world goes quiet.

---

## 📋 Format
### 🗣 Opening Monologue
Delivered from his desk with a glowing microphone and practiced smile. Warm, vaguely hopeful, always safe. Opens every night with:  
*“If the world’s still turnin’ — then I’ll still be talkin’. Welcome to Night Time, friends.”*

### ✨ Bright Spots
Footage of regime achievements across the territories. New rail lines, ration increases, community events — all curated, staged, or exaggerated for effect.

### 🎤 Guest Appearances
Carefully vetted guests — factory workers, local “heroes,” regime-approved performers — share cheerful stories that reinforce the illusion of progress and unity.

### 🎸 Live Music by *The Wanderers*
Every episode features a live musical performance by [[The Wanderers]], a beloved Disgraceland band. Their presence has boosted the show’s popularity across even the more rebellious territories.  
Morris personally recruited them, recognizing their influence. They rarely speak on air, but always close with a subtle nod to the camera.

### 🌒 Final Thoughts
Morris ends the show with a quiet monologue and a reassuring smile, sending viewers to sleep with soft words and measured calm.

---

## 📺 Cultural Impact
- **Charspire & Gutter Row:** The show is embraced as soothing late-night comfort.
- **Steeltown & Threshton:** Viewed with skepticism or irony, but still widely watched.
- Children mimic Morris’s voice. Fan art and black-market merchandise exist.
- Some claim hearing Morris’s voice helps them sleep. Others say it’s the only reason they haven’t screamed in years.

---

## 🏢 TTV Studios & Broadcast Details
Filmed live from **[[TTV Studios]]** in the heart of industrial **Gravenwild**. The building is one of the last fully functional towers from the pre-Tetra era, retrofitted with massive signal boosters and armed [[Redforce]] patrols.

The studio set is bright on screen but surrounded by soot-streaked scaffolding and smoke beyond the cameras. Power is often inconsistent, but the signal rarely fails.

## 🧠 Trivia
- Morris wears the same style of burgundy suit every night — he owns over 40 identical versions.
- [[The Wanderers]] are rumored to slip hidden anti-Duke messages into their instrumentals.
- A leaked memo once described the show as *“the regime’s velvet rope — not to bind, just to lull.”*
- [[The Duke]] has never appeared on the program.
