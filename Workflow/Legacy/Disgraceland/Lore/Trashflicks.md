# TRASHFLICKS

> [!NOTE|clean no-i right]+ TRASHFLICK
> ![[trash.png|450]]

> "Luck’s a lie. In *Sliver*, you either *bleed for your win* or you lose with style." — [[Ninepack]]

## 🩸 Overview

Trashflicks are low-budget, underground films passed around Disgraceland on scavenged tapes, discs, or crude cartridges. Screened on battered CRTs in basements, alley clubs, or projected onto ruined walls, Trashflicks are the desperate artform of a starving culture. Content ranges wildly — from salvaged pre-Tetra movies, to outlaw documentaries, raunchy comedies, staged propaganda parodies, and even whispered rumors of snuff or prophetic reels.

---

# 🎞️ Trashflick Genres

> _“The Duke’s afraid of guns in the streets, but not half as afraid as he is of a tape in the right hands.”_

Trashflicks are the chaotic, street-level soul of Disgraceland cinema. Shot, edited, and distributed by desperate hands using salvaged tech, they reflect the raw emotional nerve of a society struggling to breathe.

---

#### 🕰️ Pre-Fall Relics

**Description:**  
Ultra-rare original pre-Tetra cassettes — action movies, romantic dramas, cartoons, even sitcoms — usually warped, static-laced, or dubbed over.

**Value:**  
Extremely scarce. Most believe only **[[The Duke]]**, **[[Jacoban Clay]]**, and a few elite collectors possess intact originals.

**Popular With:**  
Wealthy traders, rebel archivists, artists hunting for forgotten truth.

**Subculture:**  
“**Tape Purists**” believe the soul of the old world lives on in its flicks.

---

#### 📽️ Disgraceland Docs

**Description:**  
Raw handheld footage and stitched-together edits exposing corruption, starvation, rebel activity, secret experiments, and executions. Sometimes staged, sometimes all too real.

**Notable Flicks:**  
- *Grain Chain* — exposes famine tactics in **[[Threshton]]**  
- *White Tiger* — rumored footage of a beast in **[[The Drownlands]]**  
- *Red Tape* — alleges **[[The Duke]]** uses doubles  

**Popular With:**  
Steeltown crews, underground educators, Widows, and rebel youth.

**Danger Level:**  
High. Possession is a crime punishable by imprisonment or worse.

---

#### 🎭 Blacktown Comedies

**Description:**  
Crude sketch reels, sarcastic puppet shows, or street acts captured during **[[Backburn]]** gatherings. Some are brilliant. Some are borderline nonsense.

**Subgenres:**  
- *Ha Ha Holes* — joke reels mocking regions or public figures  
- *Ratstick & Friends* — a filthy puppet show parodying Loyalist propaganda

**Popular With:**  
Everyone who needs to laugh to keep from crying — especially in **[[Gutter Row]]**.

**Slang:**  
**“Bloopers”** — prank reels or slapstick failures turned viral.

---

#### 🔥 Loverunners (Sextapes)

**Description:**  
The best-selling Trashflicks. Shot in secret, passed around under fake names. Sometimes consensual, sometimes questionable, always watched.

**Why They’re Big:**  
In a world starved of intimacy and driven by paranoia, voyeurism becomes currency.

**Notable Rumors:**  
- *The Widow’s Mouth* — said to feature a disguised Unseen Widow  
- *Ashmoor After Dark* — suspected Loyalist bait series  

**Trade Lore:**  
A seller in **[[Charspire]]** allegedly produced over 200 tapes under 17 pseudonyms before vanishing.

---

## 🦆 Duke-Flips & Propaganda Spools

**Description:**  
Two opposing categories — one mocking, the other glorifying **[[The Duke]]**.

- **Duke-Flips:** Overdubbed, cut-up broadcasts turned into satire or horror.  
- **Propaganda Spools:** Clean, well-produced Loyalist reels glamorizing regime victories.

**Secret Culture:**  
Some flicks are **“True Forks”** — tapes spliced to contain both a propaganda reel *and* its mockery, depending on how they're played.

**Suspected Creators:**  
Rumors point to the **Blueforce Media Division** for the propaganda, and rogue editors from **[[Ha Ha Hole]]** or the **[[Tribucia Tribute]]** for the flips.

---

## ⚙️ The Charspire Machines

**Origin:**  
A seller named **Harrel Kint** in East Gravenwild began repairing old cassette players and handheld recorders in 2175, selling them from a junk stall.

**The Decline:**  
By 2181, Kint had vanished. Official reports cite a “licensing issue.” His workshop burned one week later.

**Legacy:**  
Kint’s refurbished devices, now called **“Kint Boxes”**, are valuable collector’s items. Some say they contain hidden features or preloaded banned flicks.

## 🔟 Top 10 Most In-Demand Trashflicks in Disgraceland

These are the flicks people lie, steal, and bleed for. Some are traded like holy relics. Others are whispered about like cursed myths. Most are illegal. All are unforgettable.

---

### 1. 🎥 *Ashmoor After Dark*  
**Genre:** Loverunner / Suspected Propaganda  
**Rumors:** Said to be produced by Loyalists as bait. Features a masked man resembling **[[The Duke]]** in multiple graphic scenes. Some swear it's real. Others claim it's a test.  
**Status:** Extremely dangerous. Possession can result in disappearance.

---

### 2. 🎥 *Grain Chain*  
**Genre:** Docu-tape  
**Summary:** Documents famine enforcement in **[[Threshton]]**, including Loyalist dumping sites, corrupt crop seizures, and interviews with starving families. Credited with sparking three food riots.  
**Status:** High-priority contraband. Blueforce raids any known screening site.

---

### 3. 🎥 *The Widow’s Mask*  
**Genre:** Loverunner / Hoax Flick  
**Rumors:** A heavily stylized tape featuring a veiled woman in black performing ritualistic dances and whispering cryptic lines. Promoted as a “Widow reveal,” but almost certainly staged.  
**Truth:** No verified connection to the **[[Unseen Widows]]**. Likely a tasteless imitation or Loyalist psy-op.  
**Status:** Controversial, widely condemned in **[[The Drownlands]]**, but still sold under the table.

---

### 4. 🎥 *The Feral Tape*  
**Genre:** Found Footage / Horror  
**Summary:** Footage from a destroyed Redforce outpost. Flashes of something inhuman. Ends with a whisper over static: *“We’re not dead. We’re waiting.”*  
**Status:** Dozens of bootlegs exist. No one knows who shot it.

---

### 5. 🎥 *Duke’s Doodles*  
**Genre:** Parody  
**Summary:** A surreal animated satire of **[[The Duke]]** as a deranged children’s show host, co-starring a talking goose and haunted sink. Borderline genius.  
**Status:** Spread mostly via Ha Ha Hole patrons. Creators remain anonymous.

---

### 6. 🎥 *The Nine-Hour Kiss*  
**Genre:** Loverunner  
**Summary:** A continuous shot of two lovers in hiding — possibly ex-Wrecking Crew. Shows intimacy, desperation, and confession. Not pornographic — emotional, raw, and real.  
**Status:** Treasured and worn thin. Original tape believed lost.

---

### 7. 🎥 *Static Eden*  
**Genre:** Art Film / Prophetic Vision  
**Summary:** A disturbing montage of decaying cities, broken statues, biomech corpses, and stormlight flickers — all set to a droning hum.  
**Rumors:** Some believe it's based on a real dream. Others call it a hoax.  
**Status:** Treated with reverence by some cult circles. Illegal in Ashmoor.

---

### 8. 🎥 *Stitched Up*  
**Genre:** Docu-tape / Horror  
**Summary:** Smuggled footage allegedly from **[[Ora Razchek]]'s** mansion. Shows flickering images of reanimated bodies, mechanical organs, and stitched skin.  
**Status:** “Too fake to be true” — unless you’ve seen one of his monsters.

---

### 9. 🎥 *Red Velvet Goldmine*  
**Genre:** Loverunner  
**Rumors:** Shot in a high-rise suite in **[[Ashmoor]]**. The masked woman resembles a high-ranking official. Some say **[[Soria Langston-Clay]]**, though it's never confirmed.  
**Status:** Blacklisted by name. Copies fetch entire livestock trades.

---

### 10. 🎥 *The Day the Sky Forgot*  
**Genre:** Spoken Word / Documentary  
**Summary:** A former steelworker narrates his life before, during, and after the Tetra Bomb, over footage of the cratered ruins. Beautiful and heartbreaking.  
**Status:** Played quietly in backrooms. Always gets passed on.

## 🎬 Glossary & Slang

- **Tape Purist** – someone who believes pre-Fall cinema holds historical truth
- **Reel Rat** – slang for Trashflick traders and bootleggers
- **Tape Burn** – the distortion caused by overwriting old tapes (also slang for watching too much in one sitting)
- **Static Run** – a long night of flicks watched through haze and fuzz
- **Veed** – short for "viewer", usually used mockingly
- **True Fork** – a tape spliced to contain both propaganda and parody
