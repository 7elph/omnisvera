# [[The Silo]]


> [!NOTE|clean no-i right]+ ‎The Silo, Ashmoor
> ![[silo.png|500]]  

## Overview
**[[The Silo]]** is the towering, marble-and-steel heart of [[Ashmoor]] — a brutalist palace-turned-government fortress that serves as the official residence and command center of [[The Duke]] of Disgraceland. Rising from the highest point in the city like a monument to dominion, it casts a long shadow over the rebuilt districts below.

Once an abandoned military weapons facility from the Water Wars, The Silo was repurposed by [[The Duke]] during his rise in 2144. It is both a symbol of order and a shrine to his narcissism, wrapped in banners, statues, and surveillance.

---

## Description
- **Architecture**: Cold, brutalist structure reinforced with blastproof plating and reinforced glass. Marble columns line the entrance, stained from rain and smoke. Twin Loyalist banners hang from the central tower.
- **Interior**: Lavish, polished floors contrast with bare steel walls. Giant screens line every corridor, looping messages of "unity through strength." Secret passageways and panic rooms are built into the walls. Most who work here know only fragments of its layout.
- **Security**: Operated by [[Loyalist Guard#Blackforce|Blackforce]] and elite [[Loyalist Guard]], with surveillance drones in constant rotation. Unauthorized entry is a death sentence.

---

## Key Areas
- **The Throne Room**: Where [[The Duke]] delivers speeches and performs his weekly *[[State of Tribucia]]* broadcasts. It features a golden throne forged from melted artillery.
- **The Chapel of Continuity**: A strange, candlelit room filled with relics from the pre-Tetra era — propaganda photos, tattered flags, and the original blueprints of [[TRIBUCIA MAP]].
- **The Glass Office**: [[Soria Langston-Clay]]'s elevated, fully transparent work chamber. She can see everyone. They cannot see her.
- **The Underlevels**: Heavily restricted subfloors rumored to house interrogation cells, vaults of pre-war tech, and black project labs. Only a few living people know its full contents.

---

## Lore & Significance
- **Foundation**: Converted in 2144 by [[The Duke]] after uniting the [[Ashmoor]] factions. Became a seat of power by 2150.
- **Public Perception**: Seen as both sacred and terrifying. Citizens of [[Ashmoor]] call it "The Eye." Rumors say it broadcasts sonic signals at night.
- **Propaganda Hub**: He hosts his weekly address, the  [[State of Tribucia]] live every [[CALENDAR|Alphaday]] on the [[TTV Studios|TTV Network]].

---

## Notable Events
- **2157**: The assassination attempt by the Ashmoor Scholars — ended in the mass public execution of 43 citizens on the Silo’s steps.
- **2169**: [[Soria Langston-Clay]] appointed Mayor of Ashmoor from within The Silo after a "gas leak" killed the previous mayor.
- **2182**: Alleged quake under The Silo revealed a sealed chamber. Access denied. [[Salem Quinn]] called it "a voice buried in the steel."

---

## Secrets & Theories
- **Silo Core**: Conspiracy theorists claim the entire building is powered by a pre-Tetra AI core known as “Motherglass.”
- **Ghost Broadcasts**: Late-night static often cuts across other channels with snippets of [[The Duke]] speaking... when he isn’t on air.
- **Unseen Access**: Some believe the [[Unseen Widows]] infiltrated the Silo once. Only [[Veerah]] would know if that’s true.
