## ☠️ Description

A hulking, rust-covered monstrosity of a ship with reinforced metal plating, jagged armor, and smoke-belching engines. Black sails stitched from tarp and human leather. Deck lined with rusted chain guns, welded spears, and burning oil drums. The ship moves low and fast — far faster than it should — even through shallow waters.
