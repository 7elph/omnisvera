##  **[[Ashmoor Festival of Unity]]**

> [!NOTE|clean no-i right]+ ‎Ashmoor Festival
> ![[unity.png|500]]  

**Date:** 14th of **[[CALENDAR|BLOOMSUN]]**  
**Type:** Propaganda Celebration  
**District Held:** [[Ashmoor#Diamond Valley|Diamond Valley]], [[Ashmoor]]
**Established by:** [[The Duke]] of Disgraceland

The **Ashmoor Festival of Unity**, often shortened to *Unity Day* or mockingly called *Duke’s Day*, is an annual celebration held on the 14th of **[[CALENDAR|BLOOMSUN]]** — commemorating the official founding and “rebuilding” of [[Ashmoor]] by [[The Duke]] of Disgraceland. It marks the beginning of the modern era in [[Ashmoor]] and serves as a symbol of Loyalist control, prosperity (for some), and enforced gratitude.

---

### 🎉 **Common Practices**

- **Parades** featuring Loyalist military units, schoolchildren waving banners, and mechanized floats sponsored by Clay Industries and other regime-aligned entities.
- **Concerts** held in the [[Ashmoor]] Central Plaza, showcasing government-approved artists and propaganda anthems.
- **The Grand Address**, a live broadcast from [[The Silo]], where The Duke speaks directly to the people of United Tribucia.
- **“Unity Tokens”** — ration boosters, entertainment passes, or temporary curfew lifts handed out in richer districts, sparking envy in the poor sectors.

---

### 🏙️ **Cultural Notes**

- The festival is **lavishly celebrated by the wealthy and loyal** in [[Ashmoor]], complete with masquerade balls and private rooftop galas.
- For citizens in regions like [[Steeltown]] or [[The Drownlands]], the day is **seen as a cruel farce** — a reminder of their suffering and exclusion from the so-called prosperity.
- Despite the cynicism, **many across Disgraceland still look forward to it** as a day of relief: no work, lighter patrols, and a chance to gather, drink, and complain in peace.
