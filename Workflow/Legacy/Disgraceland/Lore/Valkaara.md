# VALKAARA

### Ancient Folklore

> [!NOTE|clean no-i right]+ VALKAARA
> ![[valk.png|450]]  

The legend of **Valkaara** originates from the old Eastern kingdoms, carried to Disgraceland by wandering traders, refugees, and black market sailors. Though few know its full origin, the name alone stirs dread in whispered conversations throughout [[Gutter Row]].

Long ago, it is said that a mighty god named **Vellokar** ruled over a distant people. His reign brought them endless harvests, strong rivers, and rich soil. But the greed of men is insatiable. In time, Vellokar's own priests and kings betrayed him, sealing him in chains beneath the earth to seize his power for themselves.

Vellokar’s only child, his daughter **Valkaara**, watched helplessly as her father was taken. In her grief, her soul broke. Her living spirit followed her father into the underworld, but her body remained behind — pale, empty, and hollow.

Her once radiant flesh turned ghostly white, her eyes drained of warmth, her breath thin like mist. Abandoned, Valkaara swore eternal vengeance upon all betrayers, swearing to roam the night and drag the wicked into the shadows, one by one. Her victims were said to vanish without trace, their souls ripped into the underworld to suffer beside her father for all eternity.

Over centuries, the legend morphed. Some claim Valkaara was no longer a woman at all, but a curse that could inhabit others — a wandering spirit of vengeance that finds new vessels when the old one fades.

### Connection to Present Day

Now, in the alleyways of [[Vileby]] and the slave routes of the [[Three Nail Market]], people whisper that Valkaara has found new flesh.

They speak of a **pale girl**, seen just before someone vanishes. She moves silently, almost gliding, her skin bleached like polished bone, her face cold and expressionless. Those who witness her rarely live long enough to tell it twice.

Some drunks say she is a demon. Others say she is a revenant of the old world. The merchants call her a curse. The gangs simply call her:

**Valkaara.**

Whether she is flesh or myth, no one knows. But each time another soul vanishes, the legend grows stronger.

### Common Sayings

- "The Betrayed belong to Valkaara."
    
- "Pale eyes mean a pale grave."
    
- "Swear no oaths in Gutter Row, or Valkaara will hear it."
