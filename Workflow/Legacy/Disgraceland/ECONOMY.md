## Currency
**Half-Slug Shred:** Half unit. Maybe buy a smoke, if you're lucky.  
**1-Slug Coin:** Base unit. Buy a drink of water, a potato, or a newspaper.  
**5-Slug Coin:** Double-thick or wire-wrapped. Gets you a solid meal or a bullet.  
**25-Slug Brick:** Two fingers wide. Fuel, a gun, or a clean bed for the night.

##  Minting & Counterfeits

- All official Slugs are minted in [[Charspire]] by the **Tribucian Forgeworks** under Duke seal.
- Counterfeiting is common in [[Gutter Row]] and some sectors of [[Ashmoor]].
- True Slugs have:
  - Standardized weight
  - Rough edge scoring
  - Indent seal of The Duke (often worn down)
