#### Omegaday, 12th of [[Calendar|Bloomsun]], 2186
> - At a [[Loyalist Guard#Blackforce|Blackforce]] press conference in [[Gutter Row]], Captain [[Kullen Dane]] provided limited updates on the ongoing investigations into the [[Gutter Row#Pisstown|Pisstown]] murders, the disappearance of [[Lash Hawkins]], and the [[Loyalist Guard]] killing in the [[Gutter Row#Red Light District|Red Light District]].
> - Tensions escalated when [[Ava Victoria]] confronted the press, leading to a brief disruption.
> - Reporters [[Rachel Gold]] and [[Marlon Lambert]] pursued leads with Ava and [[Nico Murray]], following the trail to the alley where [[Lash Hawkins]] vanished.
> - A disturbing encounter with a homeless witness revealed chilling references to the ancient legend of [[Valkaara]].
> - While searching the [[Three Nail Market]], the group was threatened by gang leader [[Samson Knight]] and his enforcer [[Jailface]], raising tensions between the [[Murray Boys]] and [[Third Row]].
> - Later, at the Murray residence, the group discussed the legend of [[Valkaara]] and its possible connection to the case.
> - In a separate development, Captain [[Kullen Dane]] received classified footage from [[General Colt]], showing a tortured Loyalist soldier confessing to sightings of a mysterious figure known only as [[The Grim Man]].

#### Restday, 11th of [[Calendar|Bloomsun]], 2186
> - At [[TTV Studios]] in [[CHARSPIRE#Gravenwild|Gravenwild]], tensions rose during rehearsal for _[[Night Time with Morris Gable]]_ as new government-mandated script changes sparked a verbal clash between host [[Morris Gable]] and house band frontman [[Jaden Kade]] of [[The Wanderers]].
> - [[Jaden Kade]] stormed off set after discovering a surprise announcement that [[The Wanderers]] would be performing at the upcoming [[Ashmoor Festival]], a move he publicly condemned as state propaganda.
> - While getting high on the roof of the studio later that evening, [[Jaden Kade]] and his partner [[Cassidy LaRue]] were attacked by the rogue killer known as [[Scumbag]]. A violent altercation ensued, ending only when [[Cassidy LaRue]] got [[Morris Gable]] and Morris fired a gun and [[Scumbag]] fled the scene.
> - [[Morris Gable]] and studio security responded to the incident, helping ensure [[Jaden Kade]] could perform during the live broadcast despite visible injuries.
> - Somewhere in Disgraceland, Scumbag nurses the wounds from his fight as he watches the opening of [[Night Time With Morris Gable]]. He swears he will get [[Jaden Kade|Kade]]
> - Meanwhile, far beneath the city, [[Lash Hawkins]], missing since [[04 - Glowing Darkness|7th of Bloomsun]], was discovered alive—trapped, restrained, and subjected to unknown experiments by [[Ora Razchek]] and [[Hella]]
