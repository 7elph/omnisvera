  </div>

<div class="timeline-item decade-2110">
  <div class="timeline-date">2119</div>
  <div class="timeline-content">
    <b>Collapse of Central Authority</b><br>

  </div>
</div>

<div class="timeline-item decade-2120">
  <div class="timeline-date">2121</div>
  <div class="timeline-content">
    <b>The Tetra Bomb is Deployed</b><br>
    The war ends not in victory, but decimation. Cities vanish. Ash falls. Over 8 billion living organisms are eviscerated immediately.
  </div>
</div>

<div class="timeline-item decade-2120">
  <div class="timeline-date">2123–24</div>
  <div class="timeline-content">

    After the bomb, sun disappears. Crops die. Temperatures plummet. Survivors crawl back into daylight as the sky clears.
  </div>
</div>

<div class="timeline-item decade-2120">
  <div class="timeline-date">2124–44</div>
  <div class="timeline-content">

  </div>
</div>

  <div class="timeline-date">2144</div>

</div>
