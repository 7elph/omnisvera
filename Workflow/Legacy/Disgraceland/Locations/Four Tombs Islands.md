# Four Tombs Islands


> *"A place of the dead, ruled by the damned."*  
> —Common saying in [[GUTTER ROW#Crossyork|Crossyork]]

## Overview

> [!NOTE|clean no-i right]+ ‎Four Tombs Islands
> ![[4t.png|400]] 

The **Four Tombs Islands** are a small chain of four landmasses located off the southeast coast of [[Gutter Row]], nestled in the shadowy swell of the [[WATER#The Dead Waters|Dead Waters]]. Though seemingly tranquil from the mainland, the islands have a deadly reputation—thanks to their only residents, the [[Rustwater Raiders]].

Each of the islands is named for the enormous, ancient tomb it houses. These monolithic structures, shaped like thin obelisks or long pillars, pierce the sky like grave markers for forgotten kings. No known historical record explains who built them or who lies within. Locals claim the tombs are ancient rulers from time immemorial.

## Inhabitants

The **[[Rustwater Raiders]]** have completely taken over the largest of the four islands. They've fortified the ruins of an old coastal **fort** and converted the still-standing **lighthouse** into a watchtower. Any ships foolish enough to drift too close are warned off—or sunk. The Raiders operate out of this isolated stronghold, launching occasional raids on the coastlines of [[GUTTER ROW#Crossyork|Crossyork]] and [[Charspire]].

No one lands on the Four Tombs Islands without permission. No one.

## Geography & Wildlife

While the islands are soaked in dread, they are reportedly lush with overgrowth and teeming with wild game. Deer, wild boar, seabirds, and freshwater springs make the land viable for long-term survival, though only the Raiders benefit from the abundance.

Many hunters and desperate fishermen have tried sneaking ashore over the years. None are known to have returned.

## Legends & Lore

- Some claim the tombs are cursed, and that the Raiders have made blood pacts with whatever lies buried inside.
- Others whisper that strange blue lights glow around the monuments during storms, and that voices can be heard humming beneath the soil.
- Old [[Gutter Row]] tales say one of the tombs isn't a grave at all—but a sealed gate.

## Strategic Significance

The Four Tombs Islands sit in an important position along the southern shipping lines between [[GUTTER ROW#The Docks|The Docks]] and [[CHARSPIRE#Broken Bones Bay|Broken Bones Bay]]. The presence of the Raiders has made this route perilous, forcing most trade vessels to reroute far north through [[WATER#Grave Bay|Grave Bay]] or risk brutal ambush.

## Known Structures

- **The Lighthouse** – Converted to a sniper post and signal tower.
- **The Old Fort** – Now the heavily guarded headquarters of the Rustwater Raiders.
- **The Four Tombs** – One on each island, made of ancient stone and towering high above the treetops.
