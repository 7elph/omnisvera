# [[Coffin Island]] 🩸  

> [!NOTE|clean no-i right]+ ‎COFFIN ISLAND PRISON
> ![[ci.png|400]] 

*Also known as: The Coffin*  
**Location:** Largest island in [[Bone Lake]]  
**Type:** Offshore Supermax Prison  
**Territorial Affiliation:** Neutral (Supplied by [[Ashmoor]], governed by [[The Duke]])  


---

## 📜 Overview  



[[Coffin Island]] is the most feared prison in all of [[TRIBUCIA MAP]]. Surrounded by the icy black waters of [[Bone Lake]], it serves as a final destination for traitors, dissidents, escapees, and anyone [[The Duke]] wants erased from history. No trial. No paperwork. Just a number, a cell, and silence.

The prison is built atop a decaying [[Water Wars]]-era military research complex, repurposed into a fortress of misery. Locals call it *The Coffin* because once you're sealed inside, you're already dead.

---

## 🔒 Structure & Design  
- **Triple perimeter walls**: Electrified, rusting, always manned  
- **Blackspire Tower**: Central watchtower and signal relay — visible from [[Burton Point]]  
- **Underground chambers**: Pre-War bunkers turned solitary tombs and "processing" rooms  
- **Open-yard punishments**: Inmates are subjected to freezing winds as discipline  
- **Single-point dock access**: One weekly barge brings food, medicine, and rotating guards

---

## 🌫 Conditions  
- Constant cold, high winds, and bone-deep dampness  
- Prisoners are **not named**, only numbered  
- **Disappearance without record** is standard protocol  
- Brutal guards, spoiled rations, no medical care unless it’s experimental  
- Prisoners fight, scream, or go mad. Sometimes all three

---

## 🧍 Leadership & Staff  
- **[[Katarina Volkoff]]** – A cold, precise woman in her late 30s. Daughter of a decorated soldier. Runs the prison like a forward operating base. Took over after the last warden was murdered in his sleep — some say by guards, others by prisoners. Rimshaw personally petitioned [[The Duke]] for the job. Rumors swirl that her loyalty came with favors, but no one dares say that to her face.  
- Most guards are dishonorably discharged [[Greenforce]] members, bitter and cruel  
- Whispers persist that [[Ora Razchek]] still pulls strings in the tunnels below

---

## 🩸 Myths & Rumors  
- **"No one escapes."** The waters are deadly, the island worse  
- The **Dead Choir**, a cult of prisoners, holds nightly rituals in the tunnels below  
- It’s said a **former Duke family member** is locked away under a false identity  
- Common saying in [[Ashmoor]]: *"Coffin’s got no door. Only a lid."*

---

## ⛓ Notable Mentions  
- **Prisoner #1412 – "Moorjaw"**: A large, quiet inmate held in the deepest cellblock. He’s survived 12 winters, multiple assaults, and even an execution order that was mysteriously rescinded. Some say he’s [[The Duke]]’s hidden bastard son, locked away after a failed uprising. Others claim he knows the truth about what’s buried under Coffin Island  
- The **Dead Choir** — cult-like inmate group living in the forgotten sublevels. They chant, bleed, and speak in riddles. Rimshaw tolerates them… barely  
- Ferry traffic to [[Coffin Island]] is managed through [[Burton Point]], with supply runs approved only by the Warden herself
