#  [[Geo Huxley|Geo]]'s P.I.


> [!NOTE|clean no-i right]+ ‎Character Portrait
> ![[gpi.png|500]]  

## Overview

Geo’s P.I. started as a favor — one missing girl, no one else looking. Word spread. Now it’s **the** place you go in [[Gutter Row]] when you need someone to find the truth, retrieve the lost, or deliver justice without waiting for permission.

Geo works alone. No receptionist, no backup. Just his reputation and whatever’s in his coat. He’s turned down bribes from the [[Loyalist Guard#Redforce|Redforce]], pissed off three regional mayors, and solved cases that [[Loyalist Guard]] "couldn’t."

---

### 📍 Location

**Crossyork, [[Gutter Row]]**  
A decaying district wedged between dead factories and rail lines. Rats run thicker than rainwater, and Blackforce rarely patrols here unless they’re dragging a body out.

Geo’s P.I. is located above **Jasko’s Pawn & Gun**, a crooked little corner shop run by a mute ex-smuggler who owes [[Geo Huxley]] his life. The office itself is a creaking second-floor unit with a cracked glass door that reads:

**GEO’S P.I. — Discreet. Determined. Dangerous.**

### 💰 Payment & Policy

- **Price:** Hefty. [[Geo Huxley]] doesn’t work cheap — not because he’s greedy, but because what he does costs. Time. Blood. Silence.
    
- **Currency:** Slugs preferred. Barter possible. Payment plans accepted if your story gets under his skin.
    
- **No Refunds.** If you lie to him, he walks. If you come clean, he’ll crawl through hell for you.
    

---

### 🕵️ Services Offered

- **Missing Persons** — the living, the dead, or the unsure
    
- **Murder Investigations** — especially the kind everyone else ruled “accidental”
    
- **Blackmail & Secrets** — identifying leaks, ghosting threats
    
- **Surveillance** — targets, buildings, caravans
    
- **Off-the-Book Justice** — if you know, you know
    

---

### 🧩 Notable Cases

- _The Thornburn Widow_ – Found her son buried beneath a Redforce scrapyard
    
- _The Weeping Mask_ – Unmasked a killer posing as a traveling preacher
    
- _Station Ghosts_ – Uncovered a Loyalist interrogation chamber beneath the Crossyork rail yard
    
- _MUTE FILES_ – Rumored job involving a missing Unseen Widow… never confirmed

### 🧃 Street Reputation

People in Gutter Row say Geo’s P.I. gets results even the Loyalists read about after. He's not beloved, but he’s respected. The desperate know his door. The dangerous know his eyes.

> “It ain’t cheap. But if Geo takes your case, you’ll get an answer. Just hope you’re ready to hear it.”
