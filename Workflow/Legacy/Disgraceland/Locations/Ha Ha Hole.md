## Overview

Deep in the twisted alleys of [[Savage Hill]], where broken signs hang like nooses and laughter usually means someone’s about to get hurt, sits one of the most unlikely cultural landmarks in all of Disgraceland — **The Ha Ha Hole**.

Opened in the 2140s by a wild-eyed maniac named [[Tyren Will]], this underground comedy club has grown from a dive for drunks to a nightly pilgrimage site for anyone desperate to laugh — or drink — their way through the horror show of modern life. With a rotting neon grin over the door and the famous slogan **“First drink is on us”**, The Ha Ha Hole is packed nearly every night.

The club draws crowds from every region — even a few brave souls from [[Ashmoor]] and [[Threshton]] — all crammed into a basement of sweaty seats, leaning in for the next punchline, heckle, or blackout.

---

## The Founder – [[Tyren Will]]

A former traveling magician, failed preacher, and once-suspected poisoner, [[Tyren Will]] is a living myth. Nobody’s entirely sure how old he is or how he managed to keep The Ha Ha Hole open during some of the darkest years after the Tetra Bomb, but he’s always been there — top hat, cane, silver tongue and all. 

Now well into his eighties, he still takes the stage unannounced on occasion to deliver bizarre rants, surreal monologues, or what he calls “anti-jokes” — strange, unsettling stories that sometimes end with a single word: *“Think.”* The audience still cheers him every time.

---

## Atmosphere

- **Stage:** A cracked wooden platform with a dented mic that sparks when tapped.
- **Decor:** Candlelight, red velvet curtains stained with gods-know-what, and every wall covered in graffiti and signatures from past performers.
- **Drinks:** The first one's free, everything after that gets progressively stronger (and less legal).
- **Security:** None. The crowd polices itself, usually with laughter or bottles.

---

## Signature Acts

- **Joley Punch** – Queen of the deadpan roast. She once told a joke so mean a Redforce officer walked out in tears.
- **Little Duke** – A parody act mocking The Duke in crown and cape. Somehow still alive.
- **Shivvy** – Combines burlesque with satire. Leaves the crowd dazed, confused, and howling.
- **Riggs Mahoney** – An ex-Loyalist turned loudmouth stage beast.
- **The Masked Heckler** – A recurring mystery comic who destroys hecklers with surgical precision. No one knows who it is.

---

## Cultural Impact

Despite being a cesspool of rebellion, filth, and unlicensed liquor, **The Ha Ha Hole** has become a cultural staple. Even citizens loyal to The Duke secretly tune into bootleg recordings of sets from the club. Its influence on slang, satire, and protest culture across Disgraceland is undeniable.

[[The Duke]] publicly despises the place and has tried to shut it down twice — once via regulation, once with a full Loyalist raid. Both attempts failed. The crowd turned violent. The club only got more popular.

---

## Rumors & Lore

- **Red Room in the Back:** Some say a private room exists where Tyren meets with outlaws, prophets, and ghosts.
- **The Laugh That Kills:** Urban myth claims one joke told in '62 made a man laugh so hard he seized and died. The tape was burned.
- **Secret Resistance:** Some believe The Ha Ha Hole is more than a club — it’s a safehouse, a spy ring, a gathering place for the loud and dangerous.
