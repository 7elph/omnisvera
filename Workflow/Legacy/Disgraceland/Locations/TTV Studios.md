### Overview

TTV Studios is the only functioning television and radio station in Disgraceland. Housed in a crumbling, retrofitted broadcast building topped by a patched-together transmission tower, it’s become the lone source of “official” news, propaganda, and nightly entertainment for the people of Tribucia. The original tower dates back to pre-Tetra times; the current crew scrounged, rewired, and extended it until the signal finally reached every region again.

**TTV** (“Tribucia TeleVision”) schedule:

| Program           | Date                              | Time              |
|------------------|-----------------------------------------|------------------------------------|
| [[TTV Evening News]]  | Every Weeknight                        | 18:00hrs       |

| [[WLL Fight Night]]      | Weekly Every Restday   | 22:00hrs         |
| [[The Duke]]'s [[State Of Tribucia]] Address    | Weekly Every Alphaday| 20:00hrs           |

The building is a hodgepodge of old tech, scavenged gear, jury-rigged generators, and blacked-out windows. On-air sets are painted to look more impressive on screen than in person — think flickering stage lights, patched curtains, and “live” audiences comprised mostly of the same few extras in different hats.

### Staff & Security

- **Manager:** [[Owen McCready]] — A burned-out, deeply private old technician, brought in by The Duke’s people for his uncanny knack for keeping ancient tech alive. Just wants to clock out, drink himself to sleep, and avoid getting dragged into the region’s politics.
- **Security:** A mix of bored Loyalist Guards and failed actors doubling as “security” on camera.
- **Production Crew:** Whoever can still operate a camera, solder a wire, or read a script.

---

### Station Manager: [[Owen McCready]]

**Bio:**  
Gruff, hunched, maybe late 50s, always in a sweat-stained broadcast cap, haunted eyes. Used to run pirate radio back in the Water Wars. Knows how to get a picture up on a broken screen with nothing but a coat hanger and a prayer. Trusts nobody, drinks nightly, and keeps a sawed-off shotgun hidden behind the filing cabinet “just in case.” Hates The Duke, but hates the alternative (unpaid work, or worse) more.

> “I just keep the lights on. What you do in front of the cameras is your problem.”

---

### Lore Notes

- **The tower** is visible from anywhere in Gravenwild — tall, ugly, patched with sheet metal and wire, always buzzing with static and the occasional power arc.
- Most citizens tune in, even if they don’t admit it. Half for news, half for a laugh at how desperate the government is to control the story.
- Occasional “signal interruptions” happen when someone sabotages the lines or when storms hit, but Switch usually gets it running again.
