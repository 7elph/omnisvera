# 📍 Three Nail Market — Vileby, Gutter Row


> [!NOTE|clean no-i right]+ ‎Character Portrait
> ![[tnm.png|500]]  

## Overview

The **Three Nail Market** is the blackened heart of commerce in [[Gutter Row#Vileby|Vileby]], a festering urban sprawl within [[Gutter Row]]. Named after the three rusted nails still embedded in the wall of the first slave-post in the region, the market operates as a brutal bazaar of flesh, chemicals, and desperation. It's a place where danger is the currency, and survival is the only reward.

---

## Structure

### 🔹 The Top Layer – *"The Facade"*
- A chaotic mix of legitimate and not-so-legitimate vendors.
- Stalls sell scavenged tech, canned goods, reworked clothing, and "imported" wares.
- Some stores are fronts for brothels, gambling rings, or backroom clinics.
- Lanterns of specific colors above doorways serve as coded signals — red for sex, green for meds, blue for weapons.

### 🔸 The Mid Layer – *"The Run"*
- Reached through alleys, basements, or coded stairwells.
- Location of most drug deals, Luxyn exchanges, illegal surgeries, and smuggling transactions.
- Slave pens are wheeled in and out under cover of tarps.
- Frequented by gangs, mercs, and rogue surgeons.

### ⚫ The Deep Layer – *"The Roots"*
- Hidden tunnels and ruins beneath the city used during the Water Wars.
- Secretive auction halls, holding cells, execution rooms, and biochemical labs.
- Ghostly quiet, except when it’s not — screams are common and ignored.
- Whispers of blood rites, body modification dens, and worse.

## Notable Elements

- **[[Luxyn]] Trade**  
  A rare and potent hallucinogenic stimulant, Luxyn is difficult to manufacture.  
  - Main suppliers:  
    - **The [[Murray Boys]]** – a local crime family with blood-soaked hands.  
    - **[[Lash Hawkins]]** – a gutsy young street dealer with a growing rep.  
  - Both do their selling in Three Nail Market, often guarded and always watched.

- **Slave Auctions**  
  Weekly events held in the mid and deep layers. Children, workers, and “custom orders” available. Desperate men and women can "sell themselves" into slavery. This system is often abused.

- **Flesh Parlors**  
  Gruesome medical dens where "doctors" perform on the desperate.

- **Merc Boards**  
  Physical message boards listing contracts: hits, deliveries, abductions, or organ bounties.
