# Chapter 07:

#### _Restday, 11th of [[CALENDAR|Bloomsun]], 2186_

With tempers high and luxyn coursing through his veins, Jaden spirals from resistance into rooftop chaos when the unhinged [[Scumbag]] crashes the night with blood on his mind.
