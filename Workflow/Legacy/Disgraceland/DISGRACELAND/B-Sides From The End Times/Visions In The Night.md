## **“VISIONS IN THE NIGHT”**  
##### _A B-Side from the End Times_

> In the crumbling cathedral ruins of [[GUTTER ROW]], the blind prophet [[Salem Quinn]] delivers a sermon of hope to his followers amidst the rising dread of Disgraceland’s unraveling. That night, Salem is seized by a terrifying vision of a future ruled by human-machine hybrids, sacred spaces defiled, and trust betrayed. Shaken to his core, he confides in his loyal companion [[Tate Faulkner]], revealing a horrifying glimpse of Tate's own potential corruption. But in the quiet of dawn, faith triumphs over fear. Salem chooses trust, affirms his belief in Tate’s goodness, and declares their next destination with unwavering resolve: an audience with [[The Duke]].
