# Chapter 06:

#### _Middleday, 9th of [[CALENDAR|Bloomsun]], 2186_

When the feared [[The Black Marauder|Black Marauder]] makes an unannounced appearance in [[WATER#Rustwater Bay|Rustwater Bay]], panic erupts—but what follows is anything but a raid. Tensions flare and strange allies are made, forcing the [[Steeltown Wrecking Crew]] to weigh principle against pragmatism. 

Across the sea of dust in the Bloodroot Canyons, #1 [[World Lucha Libre|WLL]] contender [[Bastian Redd]] is offered a chance at destiny—but at a cost that could redefine everything he stands for.

> [!world]- SPOILERS
> This chapter, titled "Showdown In Gage Park," depicts a tense encounter between the **Steeltown Wrecking Crew**, a local faction, and the pirate crew of **The Black Marauder**, led by **Captain Lyla Bonetooth**. Initially, the Wrecking Crew anticipates a raid as the pirate ship enters Rustwater Bay, but Lyla Bonetooth and Blacktide Cray instead arrive with a white flag, seeking to offload **slaves purchased by an unknown party in Steeltown from "The Duke."** The narrative highlights themes of unexpected alliances and moral dilemmas, as Jimmy Douglass and his crew, who oppose slavery, agree to take the slaves and free them, thus putting themselves and the pirates at odds with "The Duke." Meanwhile, a subplot reveals **Bastian Redd** grappling with a difficult choice: he refuses a promoter's offer to use **loaded gloves to cheat** in an upcoming fight against Bobby Cutman, emphasizing his commitment to fighting fairly.
