# Chapter 02

#### _Alphaday, 6th of [[CALENDAR|Bloomsun]], 2186_

When [[Lyla Bonetooth]] and the Rustwater Raiders are summoned to [[The Silo]] for a secret job, they’re ordered to smuggle a high-value prisoner to Steeltown — but the “prisoner” turns out to be a chain gang of slaves. As tensions rise aboard [[the Black Marauder]], Lyla must decide whether to complete the job or risk everything by defying [[The Duke]]. Meanwhile, a chilling vision haunts the [[Unseen Widows]]: a glimpse of the horrors still to come.

> [!world]- SPOILERS
> "Ghosts On The Waves," primarily details **Captain Lyla Bonetooth's critical dilemma** when she and her crew, the Rustwater Raiders, are summoned by **The Duke** for a secret job. Tasked with transporting a "high-value prisoner" to Steeltown, Lyla discovers the cargo is actually a chain gang of slaves, forcing her to confront a **moral choice between loyalty to the Duke and an act of rebellion**. The narrative intricately weaves her **internal conflict**, rooted in a traumatic past, with the diverse reactions of her crew, ultimately leading to Lyla's decisive plan to defy the Duke and deliver the slaves to the Wrecking Crew in Steeltown, a group known for its opposition to slavery. Interspersed with this central plot is a chilling glimpse into the future through **Prophet Veerah's terrifying vision** of a world consumed by mechanized horrors and destruction, foreshadowing a broader, ominous threat.
