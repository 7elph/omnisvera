# Chapter 05:

#### _Temperday, 8th of [[CALENDAR|Bloomsun]], 2186_

In the fog-choked wilds of [[The Drownlands]], twin hunters [[Raina & Risa|Raina]] and [[Raina & Risa|Risa]] complete the sacred rite to become Unseen, pledging their lives to the goddess Mirella. With [[Riley Thorne]] guiding them, they embark on their quest to become Widows and find an unlikely first kill.

Meanwhile, far from the mire, [[Ava Victoria]] searches the chaos of [[Gutter Row]] for the vanished [[Lash Hawkins]], forcing an uneasy alliance with [[Nico Murray]] as shadows gather and secrets deepen.

> [!world]- SPOILERS
> This chapter primarily follows two intertwining plotlines: the **initiation of twin hunters Raina and Risa** into the mystical order of the Unseen, devoted to the goddess Mirella, and **Ava Victoria's desperate search for the missing Lash Hawkins** in the chaotic district of Gutter Row, forcing an uneasy alliance with Nico Murray. The most important ideas revolve around the **sisters' transformation into "Widows" by pledging allegiance and consuming a symbolic "body of the goddess,"** which culminates in a ritualistic requirement to kill a male target, and Prophet Veerah's **prophetic vision of a coming war against "creatures of metal and flesh,"** urging the Widows to act. The narrative highlights the harsh realities of their world, from brutal military training under General Colt, chosen by the twins as their first target, to the cutthroat environment of Gutter Row.
