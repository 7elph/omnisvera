> [!world]- SYNOPSIS
> It’s been over sixty years since the [[Tetra Bomb]] ended the [[Water Wars]] and scorched the world clean. Out of the ashes rose **[[Tribucia Map|Tribucia]]**, a stitched-together nation ruled by one man, **[[The Duke]]**, a self-made tyrant with a god complex and an iron grip. The people don’t call it Tribucia, though. They call it Disgraceland. Six territories. Each broken in its own way. Each boiling beneath the surface. In **[[Steeltown]]**, the poor survive under the shadow of rusted towers and the protection of the legendary **[[Steeltown Wrecking Crew]]**.  In the black market hell of **[[Gutter Row]]**, gangs, slavers, and secrets rule the night. **[[Ashmoor]]** shines with wealth, built on betrayal and blood, while **[[the Drownlands]]** whisper prophecies no one wants to hear. **[[Charspire]]** is a choking machine of fire and chains, and **[[Threshton]]** hides quiet rebellion behind weathered ranch gates. As tensions rise, old factions resurface, forbidden music spreads like fire, and something monstrous begins to stir beneath the soil. Prophets dream of it. And somewhere in the static of a state-run broadcast, truth tries to cut through the noise. In Disgraceland, only the strong survive.
