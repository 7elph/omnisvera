>  Chapter 08 details from a larger narrative, plunging Captain Kullen Dane of the Blackforce into a chaotic press conference as he grapples with three baffling cases: a ritualistic double homicide, the perplexing disappearance of Lash Hawkins, and the brutal murder of a Loyalist Guard. Meanwhile, reporters Rachel Gold and Marlon Lambert, joined by Lash's girlfriend Ava Victoria and Nico Murray of the Murray Boys gang, pursue their own leads into Lash's vanishing, encountering cryptic folklore about the entity **Valkaara** and clashing violently with rival gang members, the Third Row. The chapter culminates with Dane receiving a disturbing tape from **General Colt**, revealing that a traumatized Loyalist Guard witnessed the murder of another officer by a mysterious figure known only as **"The Grim Man,"** hinting at deeper conspiracies  at play within the city's grim underbelly.
