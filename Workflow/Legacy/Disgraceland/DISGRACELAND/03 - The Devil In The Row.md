# Chapter 03:

#### _Moonday night, 7th of [[CALENDAR|Bloomsun]], 2186_

> When two off-duty [[Loyalist Guard]] take their power trip too far behind [[Hollow House]], they come face to face with a shadow the city pretends doesn’t exist. As [[Shana]] fights for survival, a chain rings through the alley, and with it, [[The Grim Man]] delivers his silent judgment. Blood hits brick, legend becomes real, and a quiet pact is sealed in the dark.

And somewhere else in [[Steeltown]], a message is delivered. Not everyone welcomes it.

> [!world]- SPOILERS
> This chapter details a pivotal night in the life of **Shana**, proprietor of Hollow House. It describes a brutal encounter where two off-duty Loyalist Guards assault her, only to be violently interrupted by **The Grim Man**, a silent and deadly vigilante who dispatches one guard and disposes of the other, cementing his fearsome legend. The narrative then shifts to **The Afterburn**, revealing Loyalist Guards delivering a suspicious invitation to the **Ashmoor Festival** from **The Duke** to **Mark Jeffrey** and **Joe Ameen** of the Steeltown Wrecking Crew, an invitation that is unequivocally rejected. The purpose of these excerpts is to establish **The Grim Man's** brutal effectiveness and the precarious, often violent, political landscape of Steeltown, where factions like the Loyalists and the Wrecking Crew operate with palpable tension.
