# Chapter 01: 

#### _Temperday Afternoon, 1st of [[CALENDAR|Bloomsun]], 2186_

> When the [[World Lucha Libre|WLL]] brings its blood-soaked spectacle to [[Steeltown]], tensions explode as undefeated champion **[[Bobby Cutman|Bobby “The Iron Bull” Cutman]]** disrespects the crowd, the city, and the **[[Steeltown Wrecking Crew]]**. As security duty turns personal, **[[Abel May Jr.]]** steps up and steals the spotlight, triggering a ferocious standoff that nearly ignites a riot. But while the world watches [[Bobby Cutman]] rage, a shadowy offer is made — and behind the scenes, something far more dangerous is watching both men from the dark. Glory, power, and legacy hang in the balance… and in Disgraceland, not every fight ends in the ring.

> [!world]- SPOILERS
> This chapter details the volatile encounter between undefeated champion **Bobby “The Iron Bull” Cutman** and local hero **Abel May Jr.** during a World Lucha Libre event in Steeltown. Cutman's aggressive taunts towards the city and the **Steeltown Wrecking Crew** provoke Abel, escalating into a near-riot and a public confrontation where Abel asserts the Crew's dominance. Amidst the chaos, promoter **Flint Garritt** seizes the opportunity to offer Abel a contract to join the WLL, recognizing his raw appeal. However, the narrative also hints at a darker, more sinister plot orchestrated by **Ora Razchek** and **Hella**, who view the spectacle as a mere distraction from their true, undisclosed agenda, suggesting a deeper game at play beyond the wrestling ring.
