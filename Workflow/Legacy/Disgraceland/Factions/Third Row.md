## Overview
A ruthless, street-born gang operating out of [[Reapton]] in [[Gutter Row]], **The Third Row** rose from desperation and grit to become the undisputed rulers of the [[Three Nail Market]]. Once just a clique of kids from Row Three, they now control trade, fear, and punishment throughout the district.

---

## 🔰 Symbols & Identity

- **Colors:** Purple and black  
  Members wear a **purple bandana** somewhere visible when in public — a sign of pride and warning.
  
- **Tattoo:**  
  A large **"III"** (three vertical scars or lines), with the word **"ROW"** carved or inked across it. Common placements include forearms, necks, and knuckles.

- **Taglines / Street Sayings:**  
  - *“Third Row rides deep.”*  
  - *“Cross the Row, dig a hole.”*  
  - *“Three nails, no mercy.”*

---

## 🧠 Leadership

### **Samson Knight** (a.k.a. **Mr. Knight**)  
A towering, sharp-minded Black man with a reputation for calm brutality.  
Street-raised and steel-spined, **Mr. Knight** unified the fractured remains of The Third Row in the 2170s after years of decline.  
Now, he oversees the entire [[Three Nail Market]] from the shadows — or from a barstool, depending on the day. His word is law.

---

## 📜 Origins & History

- **Founded:** Circa 2140s  
  By **Shad Rengal** and **Eddie Furlong**, two poor Charspire miners who worked night shifts and ran rackets by day.  
  Originally just a way to make extra coin, the gang evolved into a powerful syndicate.

- **Eddie’s Death:** Died in the 2160s under mysterious circumstances — rumored hit job over Luxyn territory.

- **The Blackshift Massacre:**  
  Shad Rengal was killed during this tragic and pivotal event. His death left the Row rudderless and vulnerable.

- **Reformation:**  
  In the 2170s, **Samson Knight** rose from the lower ranks and crushed rival cliques, uniting the remnants under a new code and strict hierarchy.

---

## 🎯 Influence

- The Third Row is the *de facto* authority inside the [[Three Nail Market]].  
- Loyalist guards and even [[Loyalist Guard#Blackforce|Blackforce]] units avoid direct conflict with them.  
- They control the movement of Luxyn (through unofficial deals with **The Murray Boys** and **Lash Hawkins**) and take a cut from every transaction in the market — legal or otherwise.
