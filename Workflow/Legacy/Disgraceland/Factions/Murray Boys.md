## Overview

The Murray Boys are the most feared crime family in [[Gutter Row]] and [[Charspire]], their influence growing every year. Built from nothing by [[Marco Murray]], the crew started with petty hustles and small-time rackets. Marco kept a low profile, never letting the [[Loyalist Guard#Blackforce|Blackforce]] cops catch a whiff of his real business.

As Marco’s [[Luxyn]] operation grew, he brought his brother Francis on board. Together, they built the Murray Boys into the go-to organization for anyone needing muscle, drugs, or a debt collected. When Mayor Cricket Granger found out about Marco’s Luxyn trade, he offered protection—so long as the Murray Boys moved product for him. Later, a similar deal was struck with Mayor Brile in Charspire, giving the Murrays control of Luxyn’s supply chain from mine to market.

Rosa Murray, Marco’s wife, manages the family’s finances and keeps the books so clean even [[The Duke]]’s men have trouble finding dirt. Their son, Nico, is just starting to make a name for himself—mostly causing headaches for the rest of the crew.

---

## Structure

- **Marco Murray (Boss):** Founder and brains of the operation. Handles major deals, alliances, and keeps the peace between Gutter Row and Charspire.
- **Francis Murray (Enforcer):** Marco’s loyal brother. Acts as the muscle—settling scores, collecting debts, and making sure nobody crosses the Murray Boys twice.
- **Rosa Murray (Treasurer):** Marco’s wife and the crew’s accountant. Keeps the money flowing, bribes paid, and the authorities distracted.
- **Nico Murray (Junior):** Marco and Rosa’s son. Spoiled, cocky, and trying to prove himself in the family business.

---

## Activities

- **Selling & Distributing Luxyn:** The Murray Boys control most of the Luxyn trade in Gutter Row and Charspire, supplying for Mayor Brile and Mayor Granger.
- **Extorting Local Businesses:** Shopkeepers and merchants pay for “protection.” Those who don’t quickly regret it.
- **Nightclub HQ:** They operate out of a nightclub in Vileby—a front for their operations and a hub for business, deals, and muscle.

---

## Reputation

Feared and respected in equal measure. The Murray Boys are known for being ruthless with enemies, loyal to their own, and always getting paid. Their network stretches through Gutter Row’s worst alleys and Charspire’s darkest corners. No one sells Luxyn or runs a racket on their turf without their say-so.
