## Biography

><h4>“People talk about luck, but luck’s just a knife in your back waiting for you to turn around. I make my own chances.”</h4>

Marco Murray grew up fast and hard in the alleys of [[Gutter Row]]. Orphaned before he hit his teens, Marco did whatever it took to keep his younger brother alive—odd jobs, hustling, petty theft, never trusting the system that chewed up his parents and spat out their bones. [[Gutter Row]]’s filth and violence became his school. Marco learned to keep his head down, keep his hands quick, and never, ever let the [[Loyalist Guard#Blackforce|Blackforce]] get close.

As a young man, Marco found his true calling in the city’s shadows. The Luxyn trade started small—just a way to put food on the table, a little light in the dark. But Marco had a knack for reading people, for sniffing out opportunities and weaknesses. In time, what began as survival turned into a business. He brought his brother Francis in when the money got too heavy for one man to carry. The Murray Boys ran their outfit smart: low profile, high profit, never greedy enough to get noticed by the big players—until Mayor [[Cricket Granger]] caught wind.

Instead of a death sentence, it turned into the deal of Marco’s life. With Granger as silent partner, Marco could scale up, spreading Luxyn across [[Gutter Row]] and beyond, laundering slugs into legitimate fronts. When Granger joined forces with Brile in [[Charspire]], Marco adapted again, managing supply lines between mines and streets with the kind of precision that made even the Loyalist Guards look clumsy.

## Personality

**Temperament:**  
Cautious, coldly logical, and slow to trust. Marco is patient and deliberate, rarely showing anger or excitement. He speaks little but always listens—calculating the angles before making any move. He treats loyalty like gold and betrayal like a death sentence.

**Vices:**  
Cigars, expensive liquor, and the illusion of control. Marco doesn’t touch his own product but spends big to look the part. His real addiction is power and the quiet thrill of beating the odds.

**Abilities:**  
Master manipulator, sharp negotiator, and ruthless tactician. He’s an expert at staying invisible—never leaves a trail, never loses his temper. Runs a tight crew and inspires absolute loyalty or total fear.

**Secrets:**  
Marco still hides money and a false identity, just in case he needs to disappear. He keeps a list of everyone who’s ever crossed him. He knows about Granger’s secret dealings with Brile before anyone else did—and quietly keeps leverage on both men.

**Beliefs:**  
He believes family comes first, no matter the cost. The world is a cruel place, and only the smart, careful, and loyal survive. Trust is a weapon, and hope is a weakness.

## Role In Disgraceland
Marco Murray is the true face behind [[Gutter Row]]’s drug trade and the [[Murray Boys]]’ growing empire. He operates in the shadows, never flaunting his power but pulling strings that reach from the darkest alleys to the mayors’ offices. No one moves Luxyn in [[Gutter Row]] or [[Charspire]] without his blessing—and everyone knows it.
