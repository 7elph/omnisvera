## Biography

><h4>“Men start fires. Women decide who gets burned.”</h4>

Rosa grew up on the tough streets of [[Steeltown]], the daughter of a steelworker and a mother who ruled the house with iron discipline. She learned to hold her own early—fighting with boys, taking dares, and running wild with her neighborhood crew. [[Jimmy Douglass]] was one of those kids—her first crush, her first fistfight, maybe even her first real heartbreak. When her family moved to [[Charspire]] at sixteen so her father could work the mines, Rosa had to start over.

Charspire was meaner, colder, but Rosa was tougher. She waited tables, hustled, and quickly learned the rules of survival. That’s when she met [[Marco Murray]]—older, sharp, protective, and with a loneliness that matched her own. They married within a year. Sixteen years later, Rosa isn’t just Marco’s wife—she’s his most trusted partner and the backbone of the [[Murray Boys]].

She handles the books, smooths over deals, keeps the officials happy, and makes sure nobody in the crew gets sloppy. She knows where every slug is hidden and isn’t afraid to put someone in their place—politely, or not. Rosa is fiercely loyal to Marco, but there’s still a little corner of her heart reserved for memories of [[Jimmy Douglass]]. Old letters, faded photos, and silent “what ifs” she’ll never speak aloud.

## Personality

**Temperament:**
Confident, poised, and unflappable. Rosa has nerves of steel and a razor-sharp tongue when needed. She’s warm and protective with her family, but icy and formidable when the business calls for it.

 **Vices:**
Red wine, classic soul records, and the thrill of keeping secrets. She loves expensive shoes and occasionally bets big at the tables—she always bets on herself.

**Abilities:**
Expert with numbers, master at reading people, and has an instinct for danger. Rosa can bluff her way through any situation, and nobody’s ever caught her in a lie.

**Secrets:**
She keeps an old photograph of her and [[Jimmy Douglass]] tucked away. Sometimes she discreetly tips Jimmy off if it won’t put Marco at risk. She’s also stashing money on the side—just in case she ever needs a quick exit.

**Beliefs:**
Family comes first. Loyalty is earned, every single day. Never let anyone see you bleed.

## Role In Disgraceland
Rosa Murray is the [[Murray Boys]]’ silent power—everyone who matters knows she’s the one who keeps the operation smooth, the books clean, and the local politicians loyal. She’s the calm in the storm and the last word in any crisis.
