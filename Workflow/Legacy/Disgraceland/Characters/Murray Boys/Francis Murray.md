## Biography

><h4>“Some folks hustle to survive. Me and Marco? We hustle so we don’t have to anymore.”</h4>

Francis Murray owes everything to his brother Marco—and he knows it. Marco shielded Francis from the worst of Gutter Row, scraping together enough to keep him clothed, fed, and (mostly) safe. Where Marco learned survival in the gutter, Francis got a taste of ambition. As a teenager, Francis went straight, working the floor at Clay Millwrights in Charspire, where his sharp mind and quick charm caught the eye of Jacoban Clay himself.

Francis rose through the ranks fast, becoming plant manager by his late twenties. He learned the art of management, the politics of the factory floor, and how to get what he wanted without making enemies. But blood is blood—when Marco’s side business ballooned into a criminal empire, Francis walked away from the Millwrights without a word, throwing his loyalty and skills behind his brother. Francis now handles the front-facing side of the Murray Boys: laundering money, striking deals, and keeping the operation just legit enough to keep the guards off their backs.

He still meets Jacoban Clay for drinks every month, spinning stories about side businesses and investments—never once letting slip that he and Marco are the men behind Luxyn. For Francis, friendship is sacred, but family is absolute.

## Personality

**Temperament:**  
Smooth, charming, and socially gifted. Francis is diplomatic, a natural schmoozer who can talk his way out of trouble or into any boardroom. He’s fiercely loyal to Marco but doesn’t let that make him naive—he’s learned to read a room and strike first when necessary.

**Vices:**  
Gambling, expensive suits, and a taste for high-stakes risk. Francis loves the thrill of outmaneuvering competitors. He drinks too much and sleeps around, but keeps these out of the business.

**Abilities:**  
Expert negotiator, accomplished liar, and financial whiz. Francis is the public face of the Murray Boys, keeping books clean, fronts legal, and guards distracted. He’s also well-connected in Charspire’s 
industrial circles.

**Secrets:**  
Francis has a private fund stashed in case the business ever collapses. He still feels guilty for leaving Clay Millwrights, especially knowing how much Jacoban trusted him. He’s also quietly tracked down their parents’ killer, but keeps it to himself.

**Beliefs:**  
Francis believes loyalty is everything—first to Marco, then to those who’ve earned his trust. He sees Disgraceland as a rigged game but believes he and Marco can beat it if they stick together and play smart.

## Role In Disgraceland
Francis Murray is the Murray Boys’ clean face and chief operator, running their finances, fronts, and public affairs from Charspire. His charm and connections allow the Murray Boys to expand without drawing heat—unless you cross him, in which case the charm vanishes and you’re left dealing with Marco.
