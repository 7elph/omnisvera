## Biography

><h4>“Relax. You really think anything bad could happen to a Murray?”</h4>

Spoiled by luxury and cushioned from real danger, Nico’s world is flashy and filled with high tastes and the kind of trouble you can usually buy your way out of. Cocky, charming, and quick with a joke, Nico has always thought of himself as untouchable—a prince of Disgraceland’s underworld, even if he’s just started learning what that actually means.

Recently, though, cracks have started to show in his golden world. Nico’s parents are slowly letting him in on the family business. He likes the taste of power, but he still treats it like a game. More interested in the thrill and attention than the danger. He boasts about things he barely understands, flirts with anyone who’ll listen, and imagines himself as the future king—even as the real wolves circle closer than he realizes.

Nico Murray grew up wanting for nothing, the only child of [[Marco Murray|Marco]] and [[Rosa Murray|Rosa]]. With two powerful parents and a taste for the high life, Nico has never known the struggle that shaped his family. He was sent to the best schools, always had the best clothes, and never missed a party worth being seen at.

## Personality

**Temperament:**  
Brash, overconfident, and witty. He’s quick to charm and quicker to show off, but deep down he’s still just a kid, a little in over his head.

**Vices:**  
Fast cars, women, gambling, and living above his means. Nico loves being the center of attention, even when it gets him in trouble.

**Abilities:**  
Naturally charismatic, street-smart (to a point), and good at talking himself out of trouble. He can be clever and resourceful when he has to be, but hasn’t faced any real consequences—yet.

**Secrets:**  
Nico’s started to notice the danger his parents face, and sometimes wishes he could walk away. He keeps a stash of cash and an old revolver hidden in his room—just in case. He’s already made promises to a rival gang, thinking he can play both sides.

**Beliefs:**  
He thinks he’s invincible, believes he’s destined for greatness, and has a soft spot for his mother, though he hides it under bravado.

## Role In Disgraceland
Nico is the cocky heir apparent of the [[Murray Boys]], strutting through [[Gutter Row]] and [[Charspire]] like he owns the place—protected by his last name, but dangerously unaware of how quickly fortunes change in Disgraceland.
