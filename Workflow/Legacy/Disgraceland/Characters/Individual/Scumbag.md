## Biography

><h4>“Every scream's got its own note, you just gotta listen close. Some of 'em? Real pretty.”</h4>

**[[Scumbag]]** is an open wound walking. Nobody knows his real name, and nobody asks. He’s one of Gutter Row’s most feared creatures — a man so utterly depraved that even the hardened slavers of Three Nail Market cross the street when they see him. He’s not a crime boss, not even a proper gang member. He’s a wildcard, a freak, and a sadist. He walks with a swagger soaked in blood and shit, because nobody’s ever put him down for good.

He’s raped, murdered, mutilated, and laughed through it all. Cops? He mocks them. The market bosses? He spits in their stalls. Loyalist Guard? He disappears into the grime like a cockroach when they come sniffing, then reappears days later like nothing happened. Some say he cuts deals. Others say he knows things. But the truth is simpler: the city’s rotted, and it lets its worst creatures live so long as they cause the right kind of chaos.

## Personality

**Temperament:**  
Wildly unstable. He swings from eerie calm to maniacal frenzy in seconds. He talks to himself, to objects, to shadows. Everything’s a joke to him — especially pain. He loves to toy with people psychologically before unleashing violence.

**Vices:**  
All of them. He indulges in sadism, rape, mutilation, torture, arson, cannibalism, and drug abuse — particularly Luxyn. He also collects “trophies” from his victims: fingers, eyeballs, tongues. Keeps them in his coat pockets like candy.

**Abilities:**  
Extremely agile and fast in close quarters. Masters unpredictability — throws off even seasoned fighters. Savvy with hiding, blending in, and escaping. Pain tolerance is off the charts, almost inhuman

**Secrets:**  
He’s killed multiple Loyalist officers, but their deaths were covered up to avoid panic.

**Beliefs:**  
He believes the world ended after the Tetra bomb, and everything now is a twisted afterlife. There is no right or wrong, just _performance._ People are meat with makeup. Pain is the only truth. He thinks [[The Grim Man]] is a bedtime story, a joke — but a _funny one._

## Role In Disgraceland
**[[Scumbag]]** is the unpunished evil that everyone endures. He haunts Gutter Row as a symbol of the regime’s failure — or complicity. His existence dares someone, anyone, to rise up and stop him. But no one has. Yet.
