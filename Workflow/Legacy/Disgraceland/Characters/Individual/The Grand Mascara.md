## Affiliation

- Title: **Grand Mascara** (157th holder)

## Summary
The **Grand Mascara** is the supreme spiritual leader of the [[El Santo]] faith, a secretive and powerful figure chosen by the High Council, known as the Senior Enmascarados, upon the death of the previous leader. His true identity is unknown to the public. He is both revered and feared.

## Appearance
- Wears flowing white and silver ceremonial robes with golden accents
- Always masked in a traditional silver luchador mask
- Draped in a dark silver cape
- Carries an amulet engraved with the sacred mask symbol of El Santo

## Known Traits
- Never removes his mask in public
- Charismatic, solemn, and unwavering
- Commands respect across all of [[DISGRACELAND]]
- Speaks with deliberate conviction
- Treats the mask as sacred — to remove it is to surrender the faith

## Beliefs and Teachings
- "To wear the mask is to carry the burden of the people."
- Preaches **strength in the face of oppression**
- Encourages followers to help the less fortunate and defend the weak
- Believes the mask makes all equal — rich, poor, noble, or slave

## Succession
- The Grand Mascara is chosen only upon death
- Selection process handled in secret by the Senior Enmascarados
- The chosen one disappears from public life and is reborn as **Mascara**

## Current Status
- The 157th Grand Mascara
- Believed to have been appointed shortly after the Long Winter
- Seen occasionally at major temples or during sacred rites
- Suspected to have political influence beyond the church

## Notes
- Rumors circulate that he was once a champion luchador before taking the vows
- Some say he speaks to the Mask of Santo itself
- The phrase **“The Mask Endures”** is often associated with his rule
