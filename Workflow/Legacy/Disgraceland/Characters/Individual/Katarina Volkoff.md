## Biography

><h4>“Mercy isn’t a weakness. It’s just an option I never choose.”  </h4>

Warden of [[Coffin Island]] Prison, **Katarina Volkoff** runs the facility with ruthless precision and cold detachment. After the mysterious and brutal murder of the previous warden, Katarina submitted her own name for the role — a bold move that caught the attention of [[The Duke]]. Rumors swirled about what she had to offer in exchange for his approval, and while nothing has ever been proven, whispers of “private meetings” behind sealed Silo doors haven't quieted since.

Raised by a career soldier in the embers of the Long Winter, Katarina never learned softness. Her father drilled into her a strict moral code, a love for order, and an understanding that power is taken, not given. She applied that teaching with brutal efficiency in every security job she held until her transfer to Coffin Island. Since taking over, prisoner death rates have tripled — officially due to “resistance” and “non-compliance.” Unofficially, she’s been purging the place.

Staff fear her. Inmates fear her more. But The Duke sees her as an asset — one who keeps his island gulag quiet, clean, and overflowing with bodies to fuel his machine. She’s an icy presence in a boiling world, and she likes it that way.

## Personality

**Temperament:**  
Hard, unyielding, and calculated. Katarina doesn’t waste words or sympathy. She commands respect through force, not charisma, and believes emotion is a liability. Her rare moments of warmth are disarming and usually strategic.

**Vices:**  
Katarina has a habit of overusing prescription stimulants to maintain alertness — something left over from her early guard days. She also has a taste for pain — both inflicting it and, to a degree, receiving it — something she keeps buried beneath her crisp uniform.

**Abilities:**  
Expert in crowd control, tactical containment, and psychological manipulation. She knows how to read a room, break a man without touching him, and turn people into informants with just a look. She’s also trained in military hand-to-hand combat and isn’t afraid to get her hands bloody.

**Secrets:**  
Katarina didn't just petition for the job — she orchestrated the former warden’s downfall by leaking his financial records to a resistance group inside Coffin Island. When the chaos started, she made sure she was nearby — the one who “discovered” the body, the one who “restored order.” She also keeps a personal dossier of intel on every major figure in Disgraceland… including The Duke.

**Beliefs:**  
She believes in strength, control, and utility. Loyalty is meaningless unless it serves a purpose. Pain is not only a teaching tool — it’s a truth serum. People show you who they are when they’re desperate. She trusts that more than any oath.

## Role In Disgraceland
Katarina oversees [[Coffin Island]], where enemies of The Duke disappear. She maintains the prison as a place of extraction, punishment, and silence. Her loyalty to The Duke is public — her own motives are not.
