## Biography

><h4> “I’ve been kissed by kings and slapped by cowards, but nobody ever left this club without feeling something. That’s power, darling, and it scares the hell outta tyrants.”</h4>

[[Tyren Will]] is the legendary founder and master of ceremonies at the infamous [[Ha Ha Hole]] comedy club in Savage Hill, [[Gutter Row]]. A towering, slender man with wild white hair and a knack for showmanship, he’s a local hero and cultural icon, often described as the heart of Gutter Row’s spirit. He opened the club back in the 2140s as a defiant beacon of free expression when laughter itself had become rare. 

Now almost 70 years old, [[Tyren Will]] remains as sharp as ever, always perched at the bar in his signature robe with a cigarette burning between his fingers, watching the next generation of comics take the stage. He refuses to leave the club even during violent crackdowns in Gutter Row. His presence is a statement. His laughter — shrill and contagious — still commands the room.

Though [[The Duke]] despises him and what the club represents, he dares not move against Tyren or the [[Ha Ha Hole]]. To do so would risk open revolt in the district. Despite persistent rumors about his sexuality, Tyren brushes off questions with a sly grin, often quipping that his “one true love is the punchline.” Whether he’s a relic from a forgotten era or the soul of Gutter Row made flesh depends on who you ask — but no one denies his impact.

## Personality

**Temperament:**  
Playful but unshakably principled. Tyren is theatrical, sharp-tongued, and endlessly curious, with an almost mystical calm beneath his flamboyant surface. He’s kind to underdogs, merciless to bigots, and impossible to intimidate.

**Vices:**  
Chain-smokes unfiltered cigarettes. Addicted to performance — can’t stand silence. Has a habit of goading Loyalist officials just to see how far he can push them

**Abilities:**  
Masterful orator and crowd manipulator. Encyclopedic memory of jokes, stories, and underground history. Has deep connections to black market information networks (disguised as comedy bookings)

**Secrets:**  
Once had a clandestine romantic relationship with a Loyalist officer who later disappeared

**Beliefs:**  
Comedy is the last weapon of the free.

## Role In Disgraceland
[[Tyren Will]] serves as the living pulse of rebellion in Gutter Row. Through the Ha Ha Hole, he nurtures subversive thought disguised as entertainment. He is proof that resistance doesn’t always come with a gun — sometimes it walks on stage in a robe and goggles and tells a dirty joke.
