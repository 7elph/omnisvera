## Biography

><h4>“I don’t need to see the fire. I trust the man holding the match.”  </h4>

Tate Faulkner used to wear a badge. Worked checkpoint patrol near the [[Ashmoor]] perimeter during the early days of the Loyalist unification. He was known for two things: following orders _exactly_, and hitting harder than a horse kick. But everything changed the day he encountered [[Salem Quinn]].

Salem was just a blind man in a red suit then — quiet, strange, bleeding from the nose. But when Salem spoke, Tate listened. The things the old man described hadn’t happened yet… but within six months, every detail came true. A raid. A bridge collapse. The death of Tate’s entire unit. All except him.

After that, Tate left his post and found the Seer. He never went back. No orders. No explanation. Just started walking beside him, watching his back, clearing the way. Over time, Tate became more than a bodyguard — he became **Salem’s interpreter**, organizer, translator, protector. He writes down every vision, studies patterns, checks dates, and warns locals when he can.

Tate doesn’t believe in fate. But he believes in **[[Salem Quinn]]**.

And if whatever’s coming is as bad as Salem says it is…  
Tate plans to meet it head-on — clipboard in hand, teeth bared.

## Personality

**Temperament:**  
Grim. Efficient. Intimidating. Speaks in short, clear bursts like he’s giving field orders. Rarely jokes, but has a dry, savage wit when cornered. Has no patience for nonsense, cruelty, or poetry.

**Vices:**  
Caffeine. Precision. Control. May enjoy violence more than he admits. Refuses to read fiction.

**Abilities:**  
Expert in hand-to-hand combat and crowd control. - Memorizes every detail — routes, rations, names, faces. Shoots to wound unless Salem says otherwise. Keeps detailed notes of Salem’s visions and interpretations.

**Secrets:**  
Was once a Loyalist security officer… until he saw one of Salem’s visions come true. Keeps a tattered photo of a young girl in his pocket — never talks about her. Once broke a man’s spine for insulting Salem in front of children

**Beliefs:**  
The Seer sees. His job is to make sure he **gets to where he needs to be.**.

## Role In Disgraceland
Tate Faulkner is the rock that follows the storm. He’s not a mystic. He doesn’t see visions. But he **believes** in Salem Quinn with a conviction that’s downright terrifying. While Salem moves through Disgraceland as a whisper, Tate is the **crack of thunder** right behind him — clipboard in one hand, sidearm in the other. He handles the world so Salem doesn’t have to.
