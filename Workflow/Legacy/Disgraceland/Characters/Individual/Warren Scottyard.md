## Biography

><h4>"Truth is like a fine suit. It’s not about what fits — it’s about what sells."  </h4>

[[Warren Scottyard]] is the dashing, immaculately-groomed anchor of the _TTV Evening News_, the state-approved nightly broadcast in United Tribucia. With his rich, velvety voice and perfect hairline, he brings calm to the chaos with every segment, summarizing war, disaster, and civil unrest with an easy charisma that leaves viewers oddly reassured. He was personally handpicked by [[The Duke]] after a private audition in The Silo — a meeting shrouded in speculation, but one that sealed Warren’s rise as the most trusted voice in the nation. Since then, he’s become a nightly presence in every home, even in the poorest districts where static-riddled broadcasts are the only source of light.

Born in the ashes of post-Tetra Ashmoor to an affluent family of land surveyors and property speculators, Warren rose through the ranks of TTV faster than most, thanks in part to his social circle. He’s a familiar face at high-society galas, private club dinners, and those strange elite functions where the wine flows endlessly and nothing is ever spoken of afterward. He resides in a pristine estate in **Diamond Valley**, rubbing shoulders with the ruling class. Despite his comfort and elite status, the public oddly still likes him — or maybe they’re just hypnotized by that voice.

Warren is close with [[Morris Gable]], and the two men are often seen together at exclusive functions or caught whispering just before air. He also shares a business-minded camaraderie with [[Jacoban Clay]], occasionally hosting “special interest” segments that just so happen to feature Clay Industries or Loyalist initiatives in a glowing light.

## Personality

**Temperament:**  
Smooth, diplomatic, and unfailingly composed. Warren rarely loses his cool and has mastered the art of being both likable and untouchable. He knows how to make someone feel seen while revealing nothing about himself.

**Vices:**  
Fine scotch, vanity, and a quiet but enduring addiction to influence. He collects rare books, not to read them, but to impress the kind of guests who would notice.

**Abilities:**  
Expert orator with hypnotic tone and perfect timing. Political survivalist — always knows the safest stance. Deep connections in TTV, Ashmoor politics, and the elite power web.

**Secrets:**  
He once broadcast a heavily edited report that led to a militia being wiped out in Threshton. He knew it would happen. He keeps a burner radio tuned to the pirate frequency of [[The Blackwire]], claiming it’s “for awareness.” He’s heard messages that rattled him. Occasionally slips messages into broadcasts — invisible to the masses, but encoded for someone.

**Beliefs:**  
Warren believes in control, in order, in the power of image. He doesn’t care who rules, so long as the lights stay on and the cameras stay rolling. Public emotion is something to be managed, like weather or traffic.

## Role In Disgraceland
The perfectly-groomed public face of Loyalist truth and civility. He soothes the citizens, comforts the regime, and keeps the blood off the news desk.
