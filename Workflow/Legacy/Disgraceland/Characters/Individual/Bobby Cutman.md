## Biography

><h4>“I don’t fight for free. I fought for survival once — never again.”  </h4>

Standing 6'9" and weighing 350 lbs, **[[Bobby Cutman]]** is a mountain in motion — feared, revered, and never beaten. Bald-headed, with a thick black beard like a wall of steel wire, he looks like he was carved out of the mines of [[Charspire]] themselves.

Signed by [[Flint Garritt]] nearly a decade ago on size alone, Cutman proved himself more than just muscle. He dominated the inaugural WLL tournament, flattening every opponent in his path. Since that day, he has remained the undefeated champion with a staggering **163-0** record.

The people call him *The Iron Bull*, and they don’t say it lightly. He moves like a freight train and hits like one too. Though beloved by fans and children alike, there’s always a hush when he walks into a room — because even in peace, he radiates danger.

He is one of the few celebrities left in Disgraceland, a symbol of indestructibility. But when asked to settle a dispute or handle a grudge outside the ring, he simply says:

 “I only lucha for money now.”

## Personality

**Temperament:**  
Calm. Reserved. Intimidating. He rarely speaks and never boasts. His silence speaks louder than any promo. He’s not interested in drama — only dominance.

**Vices:**  
Keeps to himself. Drinks expensive whiskey in private. Occasionally disappears for days with no explanation. Never answers questions about the world before WLL.

**Abilities:**  
Near-unstoppable brute strength.  Shockingly fast reflexes for his size. 

**Secrets:**  
May have ties to underground pit-fighting circuits in the early post-bomb years.

**Beliefs:**  
The ring is the last honest place in Disgraceland.  

## Role In Disgraceland
Undisputed champion of the **World Lucha Libre (WLL)**. A living myth in the ring — and a silent warning outside of it. Cutman is what happens when pain meets purpose.
