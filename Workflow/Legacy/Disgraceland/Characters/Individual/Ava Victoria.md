## Biography

><h4> "This city's full of ghosts and goddamn scum. I ain't afraid of either."  </h4>

[[Ava Victoria]] grew up where the saltwater met the sludge in [[Gutter Row]], daughter of a dockside jazz singer and a stevedore who vanished one payday night and never came back. She was raised behind locked doors and smoky curtains, the kind of kid who learned early to clock a man’s intent by the tilt of his glass. The streets gave her steel, the music gave her soul, and the men she didn’t bury gave her lessons.

By sixteen, she was running dice tables and patching up bleeding friends with a cigarette hanging off her lip. She was beautiful, sure, but more dangerous than she looked — because she knew the city _saw_ her, and she knew how to use it. She's had offers. The kind that come with diamonds, or contracts. She took none of them. Said she'd rather die on her own feet than live kneeling under someone's dream.

Now she floats between scenes — part-time bartender, full-time observer, with her ear to the bricks and her eye on everything. Folks think she’s just another local fixture. But underneath the gold and denim and smirk, there’s a fuse burning short.

The moment [[Lash Hawkins]] vanishes, she stops being background. And starts being the storm.

## Personality

**Temperament:**  
Calm until she’s not. Holds her tongue like a blade. Doesn’t lash out — she strikes.

**Vices:**  
Cigarettes. Punching men who talk slick. Running toward the gunfire instead of away from it.

**Abilities:**  
Reads a room like scripture. Streetwise, sharp reflexes, fast with a blade and faster with a lie. Can talk her way out of anything, or fight her way out if she prefers.

**Secrets:**  
Once killed a man in another city. Never told a soul. Keeps the ring he was wearing in a velvet pouch in her jacket lining.

**Beliefs:**  
You only get one shot to do right by people. Blood isn’t thicker than loyalty. And no one gets out clean — but some deserve the chance to try.

## Role In Disgraceland
Initially appears as a minor figure in Chapter 4, greeting [[Lash Hawkins]] at the pier. But when Lash disappears, Ava becomes the first to notice, the first to care, and the first to start pulling on threads. Eventually, she becomes the reluctant hero of the search.
