## Biography

><h4>“You’ve got Morris now. And as long as I’m with you… everything’s going to be just fine.”  </h4>

Morris Gable is the face you see when the rest of Disgraceland goes dark. Five nights a week, at the stroke of midnight, he welcomes the weary, the desperate, and the damned to _Night Time with Morris Gable_ — a talk show turned tranquilizer, beamed into every home by government mandate. With his perfectly sculpted hair and voice smooth as melted wax, Morris offers calm in the chaos. He never stumbles, never sweats, and never speaks a word that hasn’t been rehearsed.

His past is a carefully airbrushed mystery. Official records suggest he was once a radio performer in the Capital Crescent, though no recordings survive. Others claim he worked as a magician, a failed actor, or even a Ministry interrogator with a knack for persuasion. What’s known is that when The Duke’s Loyalists began expanding media control, Morris was already waiting — a volunteer eager to become the voice of the new age.

Morris now lives in a heavily guarded estate in **South-East Gravenwild**, one of the few clean and comfortable corners of **Charspire**. The studio itself is a concrete tower of humming wires and static screens deep in **Gravenwild proper**, cloaked in smoke and steel. From there, he broadcasts manufactured joy, one chuckle at a time, under the glowing banner of Clay Millwrights.

No one knows what he believes. Maybe he believes nothing. Or maybe he believes too much.

## Personality

**Temperament:**  
Charming, composed, unfailingly polite. He never loses his cool — not on screen, not in public, not even alone. His calm feels practiced, like a man who’s buried his nerves so deep they forgot how to shake.

**Vices:**  
Vanity. He’s obsessed with his appearance, his image, his legacy. Drinks alone most nights. Keeps a hidden stash of preserved cosmetics and vintage cologne. Whispers his own name into the mirror when he’s sure no one’s listening.

**Abilities:**  
Master of tone, pacing, and presence — hypnotic speech. Can read any cue card like it was carved from his soul. Knows how to pivot, deflect, and redirect any question. Allegedly has a photographic memory.

**Secrets:**  
He keeps a box under his bed with recordings of himself trying to tell the truth. He’s never aired them.

**Beliefs:**  
Morris doesn’t believe in The Duke.  
He believes in _order_. In _calm_. In keeping the screaming down to a dull murmur.  
He believes the truth is a poison and that people want to be lied to — kindly, nightly, beautifully.

## Role In Disgraceland
Morris Gable is the midnight voice of state-sponsored comfort — a propaganda host designed to keep the people soothed, distracted, and loyal. He is loved by many, trusted by most, and truly known by no one.
