## Biography

><h4>“I am proof that death is an obstacle, not an end. Soon, Disgraceland will understand the cost of true immortality.” </h4>


Long ago, during the chaos of the dark years, Ora Razchek was just another brilliant scientist trying to save his own skin. Working in secret, he developed a radical, invasive AI—one designed to replace failing brain tissue, rewrite memory, and eventually take over the entire mind. He called it the “Continuum Protocol.”

He tested it on himself, right before the world fell apart. The experiment _killed_ him… but he got back up. The human part of Ora Razchek died on that table, but what woke up was something else—a hybrid consciousness, a cold, calculating mix of Ora’s memories, instincts, and an ever-adapting artificial mind.

He has taken the young [[Hella]] under his wing.

**Now, Ora cannot die—** at least, not as long as his brain (and its implanted AI) remains intact. He doesn’t age. He doesn’t sleep. He barely feels pain. And as his humanity slips further away, all that remains is the drive for power, and a sick curiosity: _How many others can he remake in his image?_

### **His Mission:**

- He “perfected” himself, but now he wants to claim dominion over Disgraceland. No more hiding in the shadows.
    
- He’s building an army of AI-brain monsters, all ultimately controlled by his own will—a network of living weapons, each one a reflection of his own immortal experiment.
    
- When the time is right, he’ll unleash his “children” to wipe out the city’s leaders, topple the Duke, and take his seat as the true ruler—a god among insects.
    

### **Unbeknownst to all:**

- **Nobody knows Razchek is already dead.** To the world, he’s just a weird, reclusive old man in goggles.
    
- His army waits below, perfectly still until the signal comes—a dark, silent hive beneath the city.
    
- Only one thing can kill Ora Razchek: destroying the AI core in his brain. Anything less, he survives—rebooting, rebuilding, learning from every failure.

## Personality

**Temperament:**  
Detached. Inhumanly calm. Observes more than he speaks — if he speaks at all. He carries the unsettling serenity of someone who already knows how and when you’ll die.

**Vices:**  
Obsession with immortality, disregard for human life, compulsive need to “improve” humanity.

**Abilities:**  
Superhuman intellect, inhuman patience, neural network control over all his creations, can transfer some of his consciousness to other AI brains if needed.

**Secrets:**  
He’s already dead. If his brain is destroyed, the entire army loses connection—instant, chaotic failure.

**Beliefs:**  
Death is a sickness, and he is the cure. Humanity is a species that must evolve or perish, and he alone can drive that evolution.

## Role In Disgraceland
While every other bastard’s fighting over scraps, Ora Razchek is quietly getting ready to turn the entire world upside down. The scariest part? Nobody suspects a thing. Not yet.
