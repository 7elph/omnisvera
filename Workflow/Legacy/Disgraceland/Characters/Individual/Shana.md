## Biography

><h4>“You’d be surprised how much truth slips out when the lights are low and the drinks are cold.”  </h4>

A decade ago, **Shana** stepped into **[[GUTTER ROW]]** with nothing but a cracked mirror compact, a bullet scar on her thigh, and the quiet resolve of a woman who'd survived too many men with too many promises. No one knows where she came from. Some say she danced for Loyalists in [[Ashmoor]]. Others whisper she slit a governor’s throat with her garter clasp and never looked back.

What’s known is this: she found a rotting tenement at the edge of the Red Light District and turned it into **[[Hollow House]]** — a burlesque lounge, a pleasure den, a confessional in disguise. No neon. No stage lights. Just velvet curtains, bruised perfume, and a firm house code: _her girls come first._

She started with three women — all discarded, wounded, or in hiding. She gave them food, safety, and a cut that didn’t reek of exploitation. Word spread. Quickly. One by one, other women came — runaways, rebels, ex-slaves, ex-killers — and each found more than survival under Shana’s roof. They found **power**.

She never advertised. She didn’t need to. **Her name moved through the filth like fire** — the madame who didn’t sell her girls, didn’t let them get hurt, and didn’t care who you were if you broke her rules. Now The [[Hollow House]] is legend. Men come for pleasure. Women come for shelter. Everyone leaves with fewer secrets.

And while Gutter Row eats most who try to rise, **Shana thrives**. She’s more than a business owner. She’s **a quiet queen** in a district of rats — red curls, top hat, and eyes that see through silk and lies. She’s got dirt on half the mayors in Disgraceland, and they all know better than to come knocking uninvited.

They say The Hollow House is named for what’s left of the people who leave it.  
But those who stay?  
They don’t feel hollow.  
They feel **safe**.

And in Gutter Row, that’s a miracle.

## Personality

> [!infobox]
>
> ![[shanabeau.png|260]]

**Temperament:**  
Warm smile, sharp mind. She’s velvet on the surface, razors underneath. Knows when to flirt, when to intimidate, and when to vanish. Plays the fool sometimes — just long enough to see who’s stupid enough to believe it.

**Vices:**  
Smokes rare roseleaf tobacco. Has a taste for secrets, good wine, and expensive perfume. Occasionally disappears for days without telling anyone. Always returns sharper than when she left.

**Abilities:**  
Master manipulator, listener, and reader of people. Keeps hidden ledgers tracking debts, favors, lies — not for blackmail, but for survival. Has dirt on _every mayor, gang captain, and Loyalist officer_ who’s stepped into her lounge. Knows what’s coming before it hits the streets.

**Secrets:**  
Has an arrangement with one of the Rustwater Raiders — reason unknown. Keeps a locked room in the back of the lounge. No one enters but her.

**Beliefs:**  
Men think they run the world. They don’t even run their own hands.

## Role In Disgraceland
Shana is the flame at the center of the Red Light District — the woman everyone underestimates until it’s too late. She owns and runs the most notorious, tightly controlled burlesque lounge in Gutter Row — a place where bodies dance and lips loosen. She’s not a spy, but she knows **everything**. And that’s why she’s still breathing.
