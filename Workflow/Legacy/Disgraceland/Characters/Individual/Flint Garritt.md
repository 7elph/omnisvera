## Biography

><h4>“Lucha's happen all over. We might as well sell some tickets to it.”  </h4>

[[Flint Garritt]] is the owner of one of the largest mines in [[Charspire]], with his reach extending through scrapyards, foundries, and industrial facilities all across [[TRIBUCIA MAP]]. Everything falls under his banner: **FlintCo** — an empire built from slag, sweat, and grit.

He's been locked in bitter competition with [[Jacoban Clay]] for decades. The two men dominate the industrial elite of Disgraceland, yet can barely stand to be in the same room. Clay builds for power and profit; Garritt builds for legacy. They both know there’s only room for one king.

A lifelong fan of **Lucha Libre**, Flint revived the long-dead [[World Lucha Libre]] ten years ago, believing that Tribucia’s beaten-down population needed more than just food and fuel — they needed something to believe in. Under his leadership, WLL rose from the ash like an old god, draped in masks and glory. It quickly became one of the only remaining spectacles left in Disgraceland, a roaring echo of the world that was.

## Personality

**Temperament:**  
Bold and brutal. Charismatic when it suits him, but quick to anger. He commands like a general and negotiates like a gambler. He’s not interested in being liked — he’s interested in being remembered.

**Vices:**  
Smokes too much, drinks even more. Keeps company with fighters, ex-cons, and steelworkers. Rumors swirl about an underground pit-fighting league run through his scrapyards. Probably true.

**Abilities:**  
Expert negotiator and industrial tactician. Has a near-photographic memory for names, debts, and deals. Trained wrestler in his youth — still spars in private.

**Secrets:**  
His end goal is to get Jacoban Clay into the ring.

**Beliefs:**  
Power earned through legacy is stronger than power taken through fear.

## Role In Disgraceland
Industrial titan and cultural revivalist. Flint Garritt is the founder of **FlintCo** and the man responsible for resurrecting **World Lucha Libre (WLL)** — giving the people of Tribucia their fight back.
