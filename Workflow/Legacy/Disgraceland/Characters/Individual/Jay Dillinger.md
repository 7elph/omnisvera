## Biography

><h4>"They can’t hit what they can’t catch — and they sure as hell can’t catch me."  </h4>

Born in the soot-choked backstreets of [[Steeltown]], **[[Jay Dillinger]]** was a flash of motion in a city built on rust. Raised by his older sister after their parents vanished working the mills, Jay grew up hustling, betting, and dodging trouble — usually faster than anyone else. By twelve, he was already a street legend, running pickup [[Poundball]] games against grown men twice his size. By sixteen, he was drafted into the [[Steeltown Wreckers]] after leaping a wall mid-game and scoring blind off a ricochet.

Jay's small frame and explosive speed earned him the name _Slipghost_, a phantom on the field who could twist through cracks in the line like water through busted concrete. Fans say he doesn’t run — he glides. His signature plays blur by in seconds, but the fallout lasts much longer. Opposing defenders end up tasting gravel, and goalies often get nothing but wind in their face.

He plays with swagger, a chipped grin under his dented helmet, and always seems one step ahead — not just of defenders, but of fate itself. Whether he's flipping the ball off his elbow into the trashcan or slipping through a wall of bodies untouched, **[[Jay Dillinger]]** plays like the whole damn world’s watching, and he’s got something to prove.

## Personality

**Temperament:**  
Charming, fast-talking, and cocky, but rarely cruel. Lives for the spotlight. Keeps his cool under pressure and has a wicked sense of humor, even in the middle of a brawl.

**Vices:**  
Gambles compulsively — not just coin, but dares, challenges, even secrets. Drinks rustshine before games as a “luck charm.” Addicted to adrenaline. Craves danger off the field almost as much as on it.

**Abilities:**  
Unmatched agility and foot speed. No one cuts or jukes like Jay. Ridiculous spatial awareness — can pass or score from angles that seem impossible. Plays like he has eyes in the back of his head and springs in his knees. Elite trash-talker. Throws defenders off before he even moves.

**Secrets:**  
Carries a crumpled, water-stained letter from his father — found years after his disappearance. Never told his sister. Is partially deaf in one ear from a blow he took during a Wreckers riot. Hides it well.

**Beliefs:**  
Thinks Poundball is the only honest thing left in Disgraceland.

## Role In Disgraceland
Jay Dillinger is a folk hero to the undercity. He represents hope, rebellion, and survival — a living reminder that sometimes the little guy not only survives, but wins. He’s the fastest damn thing in a place where nothing moves unless it’s burning.
