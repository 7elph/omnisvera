## Biography

><h4>“Tell your boss if he wants a real beating, he can come down here himself. I’ll leave the lights on.” </h4>
 
Ray Hawkins, better known as “Lash” on the street, grew up in the crumbling tenements of [[Gutter Row]], dodging debt collectors and guards since he was old enough to run. Unlike the kids with powerful families, Lash fought for every inch—learning early that if you wanted to survive, you needed to hit fast and never back down.

Lash started small, hustling stolen goods and fake IDs before cooking up his own [[Luxyn]] recipe. A frequent customer of [[Hollow House]], His product hit the streets strong, cleaner and safer than the [[Murray Boys]]’ usual cut, and word spread quick. Dealers lined up, and the cutthroat corners of [[Charspire]] began to shift. He made just enough enemies to stay sharp, but just enough friends to never be alone.

[[Nico Murray]] tried to flex on him once—showed up with an entourage, mouthing off in front of a crowd. Lash didn’t even let him finish talking. The beating was quick, public, and left Nico bleeding and humiliated in front of his date and his friends. Since then, Nico’s hated him, but can’t touch him—the product is too good and the streets too loyal.

## Personality

**Temperament:**  
Wiry, quick-witted, and unshakable. Lash doesn’t bluff—he moves first. He laughs off threats and never overplays his hand.

**Vices:**  
Card games, cheap whiskey, and picking fights with people who think they’re untouchable.

**Abilities:**  
Fast fists, a brilliant cook when it comes to [[Luxyn]], and a master at turning a crowd in his favor. He can take a punch, but usually doesn’t have to.

**Secrets:**  
Keeps his family out of Gutter Row, sending most of his earnings home. He’s got an escape plan if things ever get too hot. Secretly respects Marco Murray, but will never admit it.

**Beliefs:**  
You make your own luck. Loyalty is earned, never bought. The best way to win is to make them laugh before you lay them out.

## Role In Disgraceland
Ray “Lash” Hawkins is the upstart threat shaking up [[Charspire]]’s [[Luxyn]] market—outshining the [[Murray Boys]] on their own turf, and daring anyone to take him down.
