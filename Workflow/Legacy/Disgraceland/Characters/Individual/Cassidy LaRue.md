## Biography

><h4>“Fuck you, you bitch! You filthy little gutter rat!” - Cassidy to Soria Langston-Clay  </h4>

Cass LaRue is the last girl you’re supposed to bring home and the first to drag you out when you should be sleeping. She’s got peroxide-blonde hair hacked off at the chin, eyes like melting chrome, and a laugh that always sounds a little too close to screaming. Born in the gutter-alleys of Gutter Row, she started out running amphetamines for the Calavera Union and wound up singing backup in backroom bands that played for liquor and leftover pills. Cass’s voice is sweet, but her mouth is poison — she’s never learned to back down, never cared to apologize, and never let heartbreak slow her down for more than a night.

Cass and Jaden met at a blackout party in a half-collapsed speakeasy, and by dawn they’d broken up twice, kissed four times, and stolen a cop’s boots together. She moves through his life like a storm: always one step ahead of the law, always one drink away from disaster, always looking for the next thrill. She’s Jaden’s biggest mistake and his favorite muse. She feeds his wildest instincts — and he’s never written a better song since she showed up. Some say she’s trouble, some say she’s cursed, but nobody ever forgets her.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Chaotic, impulsive, fearless. Cass laughs at danger, spits at authority, and treats every moment like it’s her last. She’s volatile as hell but can turn soft and affectionate in private — especially when she thinks nobody’s looking. Wildly protective of her friends, but just as likely to screw them over if the mood suits her.

**Vices:**  
Anything that burns, buzzes, or blurs the world — liquor, pills, cheap amphetamines, and raw adrenaline. Gets off on breaking into forbidden places. Never met a drug she wouldn’t try once. Loves starting fights with people twice her size.

**Abilities:**  
Sharp-tongued singer with a voice that can flip from velvet to razor wire. Expert at sneaking, lying, and finding hidden ways into any building in Disgraceland. Knows every fence, runner, and dealer from Steeltown to Charspire. Plays dirty harmonica, mean backup guitar, and can turn a riot into a party — or the other way around.

**Secrets:**  
Cass once double-crossed the Calavera Union on a major drug deal and barely escaped with her life. She’s living under a fake name, and there’s still a bounty out for her — only a handful know her real past. She’s haunted by a year she can’t remember, spent in some “clinic” outside Ashmoor — and every so often, she has gaps in her memory she can’t explain.

**Beliefs:**  
The world is doomed, so live while you can. Trust no one, especially lovers, but die for your friends if you have to. Music is the last honest thing left in Disgraceland, and every note is a middle finger to The Duke. If there’s a hell, it probably looks a lot like home.

## Role In Disgraceland
Cass is the fire Jaden can’t quit — muse, partner-in-crime, and sometimes the only thing keeping him from burning out for good. Together, they’re the heartbeat and headache of Disgraceland’s outlaw scene, legends in the making, or on their way to self-destruction.
