## Biography

><h4>"The land remembers. And so do I."</h4>

Nita Blackbird was born into a resilient Indigenous community along the remnants of the Sinner's Sea, a people who held on to their traditions even as the world crumbled around them. She was taught to read the land, track game, and endure the harsh realities of a fractured world. Her family believed that survival wasn’t just about strength — it was about remembering who they were.

That belief shattered the day the Duke’s enforcers came. They razed her village, claiming it was harboring rebels. Nita’s parents were killed in the slaughter, and her younger brother was dragged away in chains. Left for dead, Nita vowed to one day find him or at least make those responsible pay.

For years, she wandered the desolate roads of Disgraceland, surviving through her skills and the whispers of the land. Her fierce reputation eventually caught the attention of the Unseen Widows. Veerah, the enigmatic leader, offered Nita a place among them — not just as a warrior, but as a symbol of retribution. The Widows moved like shadows, assassinating those who served the Duke and liberating those who suffered under his rule.

Nita quickly became one of Veerah’s most trusted operatives. Her ruthless efficiency in the field, combined with her quiet compassion for the innocent, earned her respect within the ranks. Despite her loyalty, however, doubt lingers. While Veerah is revered as a prophet, Nita remains skeptical of her visions. The certainty with which Veerah commands the Widows unnerves her. Nita follows orders, but she watches — questioning whether the ends truly justify the means.

Her gold bird emblem, stitched over her heart, serves as a reminder of her family and the freedom she fights for. Every mission she undertakes is another step toward avenging the past and preventing others from suffering the same fate. But Nita knows that one day, her loyalty to the Widows and her personal quest for justice may collide.

The question is — when that day comes, which path will she choose?

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Nita is stoic, grounded, and fiercely intuitive. She speaks with purpose, rarely wastes breath, and moves through the world like she’s always listening for something most people can’t hear. She is protective, introspective, and commands respect with quiet authority.
**Vices:**  
Obsessively carves notches into her gear — one for every life taken in her pursuit of justice. Refuses to stay in one place for too long. Prone to disappearing for days at a time to "clear her head."
**Abilities:**  
Master tracker and wilderness scout. Exceptional with bows, blades, and snares. Reads terrain and movement like second nature. Knows when she’s being watched. Can move silently for miles. Deeply tuned to her surroundings.
**Secrets:**  
She still believes her brother may be alive — and has followed rumors of him to places even the Widows avoid. Keeps a small carved animal figure from her childhood hidden in her satchel.
**Beliefs:**  
Believes in justice more than vengeance, but often confuses the two. Skeptical of Veerah’s divine gift — she follows her out of loyalty and shared purpose, not blind faith.

## Role In Disgraceland
Nita serves as the Unseen Widows' top scout, hunter, and survivalist. When a mission demands traversing dangerous terrain, uncovering hidden camps, or striking from the wilderness, Nita is sent. Her ability to stalk a target unseen across miles of broken country has earned her a ghostlike reputation. Veerah trusts her instincts, even if their beliefs don’t align. Among the Widows, Nita is both a warrior and a guardian — a symbol that there’s still honor in the fight. But her soul is divided, caught between duty to the Widows and a personal promise to a family she may never see again.
