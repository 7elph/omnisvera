## Biography

><h4>"I heard her speak. And I knew everything else had to die."</h4>

**Len Vyne** once wore the black and gray of the **Loyalist Guard**, a rising star in **[[Loyalist Guard#Blackforce|Blackforce]]** assigned to strategic recon and field elimination. Cold, efficient, and utterly loyal, she carried out assassinations in the name of the Duke without question. Until the day she crossed paths with a woman in white, veiled in dusk, speaking softly to a crowd of the broken.

**Veerah.**

Something changed in Len that night. She claimed it wasn't madness or guilt — it was clarity. In Veerah’s voice, she heard not rebellion but truth. A truth so sharp it cut through years of training, doctrine, and blood. That same night, Len vanished from Loyalist records. Her barracks burned. Her commanding officer was found with his throat slit and his eyes removed.

Weeks later, she appeared in the Drownlands, dagger in hand, kneeling before Veerah.

Since then, **Len has served as one of the most lethal Widows**, a zealot whose devotion borders on religious obsession. She doesn’t kill for coin or politics. She kills for **Veerah**. She believes the Prophet’s visions are law — not prophecy, but premonition. And she’ll do anything to protect the woman who set her soul on fire.

Her past as a Loyalist agent makes her dangerous beyond measure. She knows how the Guard thinks. She knows how they *fail*. She’s used every tactic she once taught others to break the back of the very system she helped uphold.

To some Widows, she’s an asset. To others, she’s terrifying. To Veerah, she’s proof that even shadows can be reborn in the light.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Len is quiet but intense, often staring through people as if she’s dissecting their soul. She rarely speaks unless it’s about Veerah, and when she does, it sounds like scripture. She follows orders exactly — unless she thinks she knows better. Which she often does.
**Vices:**  
Tattooed a kill tally from her Loyalist days across her ribs — refuses to remove it. Keeps her old uniform folded beneath her Widow garb, as a reminder. Occasionally “disappears” former Guard members she deems irredeemable, without permission.
**Abilities:**  
Elite infiltration, psychological warfare, knowledge of Loyalist formations and tactics. Expert in silent takedowns, blade throwing, and pressure point combat. Can mimic Loyalist command codes to breach secure locations.
**Secrets:**  
She still carries her Loyalist ID tag in a leather pouch around her neck. Talks to it before missions like it’s a penance. Some believe she’s heard Veerah's voice in her dreams — even when the Prophet sleeps soundless.
**Beliefs:**  
Absolute faith in Veerah as a divine instrument. Sees the Faith of Mirella not as a religion but as a *directive*. Believes killing in Veerah’s name is a holy act.

## Role In Disgraceland
Len is the Widow that slips through enemy lines before the rest even arrive. Her past as a Loyalist operative gives her insider knowledge that makes her indispensable — and unsettling. She is often dispatched alone, trusted to dismantle Loyalist strongholds from the inside out. To the Widows, she’s both weapon and warning: proof that the Duke's servants can be turned — and turned into nightmares.
