## Biography

><h4>“You’ll break before I bleed.”</h4>

Darius Adeyemi doesn’t walk — he crashes. Into rooms, into fights, into lives. He’s the first one over the railing when the _Black Marauder_ grapples onto a target ship, and the last one to stop swinging when the blood settles. But he has a heart.

They call him the **Wild Man**, and they mean it. Darius fights like a force of nature — raw, chaotic, and terrifyingly efficient. Jagged bracers, iron hooks, busted teeth, a roar that echoes across decks — he doesn’t need strategy. He needs momentum. And rage.

But under the carnage is a man loyal to the core. Loyalty is his creed. You earn it, and he’ll rip the world open for you. Break it, and he’ll drown you in your own blood.

His sister, **Zadie**, is the only one who can hold that storm together. She doesn’t tame him — she tunes him. The two move like they share a heartbeat. Fire and flint. Explosive when sparked.

The legend goes: once, in the slave pits of Gutter Row, a chained fighter bit a man’s throat open and walked through fire without blinking. That was Darius. Or maybe it wasn’t. But no one questions it.

There’s something cracked inside him — something deeper than violence. He mutters in dead languages, sees things no one else does. The Tetra blast might’ve broken something in his brain… or awakened something else. Either way, he doesn’t talk about it. But Zadie knows. And it scares her more than his fury ever could.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Volatile and feral. Darius doesn’t ease into situations — he explodes into them. Laughs during fights. Goes still when others panic. He’s a storm with bones.
**Vices:**  
Violence is comfort. Fighting keeps the voices down. He drinks, fights, and chases chaos — not for pleasure, but because stillness is worse.
**Abilities:**  
A brutal brawler with unmatched strength and reflexes. First to board. Last to fall. Pain doesn’t slow him — it fuels him. Whispers claim he doesn’t sleep, just “shuts down.”
**Secrets:**  
He hears things — whispers under the waves, shadows crawling at the edge of his sight. He’s never said what they are. But Zadie’s heard enough to know they’re real. And dangerous.
**Beliefs:**  
Loyalty above all. Not to titles or factions — to people. Betray him, betray his crew, betray his sister… and pray the lake swallows you before he finds you.

## Role In Disgraceland
**Darius Adeyemi** is the black iron engine of the _Black Marauder_. When negotiation fails and fear doesn’t work, Lyla points, and Darius breaks the world open. He’s not the warning — he’s what comes after.
