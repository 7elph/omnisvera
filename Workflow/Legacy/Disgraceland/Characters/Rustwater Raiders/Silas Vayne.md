## Biography

><h4>“I’ve slit more throats with a whisper than most men do with steel.”</h4>

Silas Vayne is the whisper behind the waves, the man who can make a threat sound like a promise and a death sentence feel like a business deal. Smooth Silas, they call him — not just for his voice, but for how he glides through chaos like a shadow with a smirk.

Born to a pair of South American traders who danced the line between legal commerce and smuggling, Silas grew up bartering his way through ports before he ever swung a blade. By the time the Tetra bomb brought fire to the sky, he already knew that survival wasn’t about strength — it was about charm, leverage, and knowing who to betray at the right time.

The collapse brought him north, where calmer waters held sharper sharks. He met Lyla Bonetooth aboard the future _Black Marauder_, recognizing a fellow predator with eyes on the helm. When the mutiny came, it was Silas who whispered discontent into the ears of the weak and fearful, making sure their knives were ready before Lyla ever gave the signal. He didn’t just back the rebellion — he orchestrated half of it from the shadows.

Now, as her right hand, Silas Vayne is the crew’s voice in foreign ports and backroom deals. Where Lyla enforces order through dread, Silas ensures respect is paid with interest. He smooths over the messes, cuts the final throats, and keeps the crew in line with grins that slice like razors.

Silas is a vision of calculated chaos — tattoos sprawled across his chest and arms, waves and serpents winding around a ship caught in a storm. He wears a red bandana slicked with sea sweat and a spiked leather coat that creaks with menace. The man is a contradiction: warm and venomous, inviting and dangerous.

He is loyal to Lyla, for now. But ambition roils just beneath the surface. He’s running cons on everyone — even her. Because Silas doesn’t serve forever. He builds empires from whispers, and one day soon, he might just crown himself king of the tide.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Charming, slippery, and unreadable. Silas turns tension into opportunity and never says more than he must. If he's smiling, you're already playing his game.

> [!infobox]
>
> ![[rsl.png|260]]

**Vices:**  
He’s addicted to secrets and the thrill of the con. Lies spill from him easier than breath. He skims from every deal, flirts with betrayal, and gambles with more than gold.

**Abilities:**  
An unmatched manipulator. Whether charming a room or dismantling a mutiny with one whisper, Silas moves unseen until it’s too late. He fights with precision but prefers never to draw steel — words, to him, are sharper.

**Secrets:**  
He’s trading secrets behind Lyla’s back — with Loyalist defectors, rogue Raiders, even old Wreckers. There’s a long con at play, and only Silas knows where it ends.

**Beliefs:**  
Control. It doesn’t matter who commands the ship, so long as Silas is the one steering its fate from behind the curtain.

## Role In Disgraceland
**Silas Vayne** is the voice of the _Black Marauder_, the smirk behind the steel. While others fight and bleed, he’s the one shaking hands, signing deals, and deciding who gets to drown. Without him, the Raiders lose their edge.
