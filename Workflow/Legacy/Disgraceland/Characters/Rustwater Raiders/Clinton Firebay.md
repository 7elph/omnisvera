## Biography

><h4>“I smile so I don’t burn the world down. Don’t give me a reason to stop.”</h4>

Clinton Firebay wears his charm like armor — a grin too easy, a gaze too knowing. He’s got the swagger of a man who’s outrun death and the scars to prove it. Tall, broad, and sun-bronzed, his windswept blond hair and deep-set eyes catch attention before he ever speaks. But make no mistake — the fire in him isn’t for show. It’s real. And it’s dangerous.

Raised on the salt-heavy winds of Rustwater Bay, Clinton’s earliest memories are waves and fishnets, learning the pulse of the water from a father who treated the lake like scripture. But after the Tetra bomb turned the skies black and the coasts into ash, Clinton lost everything. His family, his home, and his purpose — swallowed by fire.

Something dark took root in him that day. His temper, once manageable, turned into a storm. He drank too much, fought too often, and landed in too many jails. That’s where he met [[Silas Vayne]] — a mirror in many ways. Silas saw the burn in Clinton and chose to steer it, not snuff it. The two became thick as blood, and when Silas joined the [[Rustwater Raiders]], Clinton followed.

Now a key figure aboard the _Black Marauder_, Clinton is their master of tides — able to sense shifts in current like a sixth sense. Some say he was born with the lake in his blood, others whisper it’s something darker. He can thread a pirate ship through needle-thin ruins and steer blind through a fog no compass can read.

But the man beneath the charm is fraying. Clinton tries to laugh, flirt, and drink the pain away. He hides behind bravado. But when that mask cracks, he becomes something else — a weapon. And that weapon doesn't stop until everything around it is broken.

He’s older than most on the ship and remembers the world before. He pretends it doesn’t haunt him — but it does. The only tether he has left is Silas, who seems to be the only one that can reach through the fire and pull him back.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Charismatic and easygoing until his fuse hits the end. Clinton talks like a poet but explodes like a cannon.
**Vices:**  
Rage, liquor, guilt — his holy trinity. He can’t stop smiling because he knows what happens when he doesn’t.
**Abilities:**  
Unparalleled navigator of Rustwater Bay and Gravelake. Feels the lake like a second skin. Also vicious in hand-to-hand combat — especially if cornered.
**Secrets:**  
A box of his father’s waterlogged journals lies hidden beneath a floorboard near the galley. He won’t read them. But he also won’t throw them away.
**Beliefs:**  
Forget the past. Burn it. The only way forward is with the wind at your back and no map in your hands.

## Role in Disgraceland
**Clinton Firebay** is the _Black Marauder’s_ guiding wind and secret storm. He is the beating heart of the crew’s direction — both their compass and their chaos.
