## Biography

><h4>“If you’re playing the game right, they’ll never see the knife till it’s buried in their ribs.”</h4>

Zadie Adeyemi doesn’t need to shout. When she speaks, the room listens — not out of fear, but because it knows she’s already done the math on how to burn it down. The Black Marauder runs on iron, blood, and fire… and Zadie keeps it all balanced.

Born in the rust-riddled ruins of Disgraceland’s eastern edge, she survived on wit, weaponry, and weaponized charm. Now she’s Quartermaster of the Raiders — the one who tracks every bullet, blade, and bottle aboard the ship, and ensures every raid ends with profit, not excuses. She’s Lyla’s shadow strategist, a storm in a silk coat, and the one crewmembers fear disappointing more than the Captain herself.

Zadie’s got eyes on every angle. When she negotiates, you don’t realize you’ve lost until she’s already two ports away. And behind that leather-bound journal she always carries? Blueprints. Names. Vengeance. Maybe even futures she’s yet to cash in.

Her loyalty to Lyla Bonetooth is firm. For now. But make no mistake — Zadie is nobody’s pawn. If the tides shift, she’ll already be standing where the water breaks.

The only person who gets past her armor is her brother, **Darius**. She doesn’t speak softly to him — she commands him the same as anyone else. But the bond between them is deep and ancient. She’s his calm. He’s her chaos.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Commanding, poised, and calculating. Zadie doesn’t raise her voice — she silences others with presence alone.
**Vices:**  
Control. She craves leverage and influence the way others crave comfort. Secrets are her currency, and power is her drug of choice.
**Abilities:**  
Master of logistics, tactics, and psychological warfare. Zadie can plot a raid, flip a mutiny, or collapse an enemy faction without raising her blade — though she’s deadly with it too.
**Secrets:**  
Her journal is rumored to contain blackmail, backup plans, hit lists — even post-Lyla contingencies. Nobody knows how deep it goes.
**Beliefs:**  
Survival is earned. Loyalty is negotiable. And power belongs to the one willing to play the long game.

## Role In Disgraceland
**Zadie Adeyemi** is the spine of the Black Marauder. While others shout, bleed, or threaten — she plans. She executes. And she wins. She’s not the next in line… she’s the one building the throne.
