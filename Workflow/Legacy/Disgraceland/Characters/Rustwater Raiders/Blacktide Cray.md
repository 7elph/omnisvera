## Biography

><h4>“If I have to explain the rules, you’re already too close to breaking them.”</h4>

Blacktide Cray is a man who commands attention without ever needing to ask for it. Towering over most of the crew, his massive frame and unrelenting presence make him a force to be reckoned with. Broad-shouldered, with a thick beard that frames his scarred face, he rarely speaks, allowing his sheer presence to do the talking. But when he does break his silence, his voice rumbles like distant thunder. Always has his trusty parrot side-kick, Parrot, with him.

No one aboard the [[The Black Marauder]] truly knows where Cray came from, though the whispers follow him like a shadow. Some say he was once a slave trader, a man who profited off misery and chains. Others claim he hated the life forced upon him, driven by the cruelty of the trade until the day he snapped—killing his own master and setting those in bondage free. Whether the rumors are true or merely fragments of fearful speculation, Cray does not say. And no one dares ask.

His path crossed with Capt. [[Lyla Bonetooth]] years ago, long before the [[Rustwater Raiders]] earned their fearsome reputation. It was in a grimy tavern along the [[WATER#Rustwater Bay|Rustwater Bay]] where Cray's stoic demeanor cracked for the first time. A drunken brawl erupted, and Lyla, ever one to challenge danger head-on, found herself outnumbered. Without hesitation, Cray intervened. The giant’s fists struck like hammers, and the chaos ended almost as quickly as it had begun. When the dust settled, Lyla offered him not only a drink but a job. She saw in him something few others could—a man searching for purpose. Cray accepted without a word.

Now the ship’s first officer, Cray serves as the solid backbone of [[the Black Marauder]]. He enforces [[Silas Vayne]]'s orders and ensures Lyla’s commands are followed without question. Though Silas is the ship’s voice and cunning hand, Cray is the muscle that ensures loyalty. His judgment is rarely questioned, and when it is, the dissent seldom lasts long.

Despite his fearsome reputation, there is one soul aboard [[the Black Marauder]] who can break through Cray’s hardened exterior—his parrot, Jackie. The vibrant green bird perches proudly on Cray’s shoulder, screeching obscenities in a voice that echoes across the deck. No one knows where Jackie learned to curse with such imaginative flair, though many suspect Silas had a hand in it. Regardless, the parrot is as much a part of the crew as any pirate, and Cray’s gruff affection for the foul-mouthed creature is undeniable.

While the truth of Blacktide Cray’s past remains a mystery, his loyalty to [[Lyla Bonetooth]] is unquestionable. He fights not for gold or glory, but for the belief that the Black Marauder gave him something no other ship or shore could—a second chance. And so he sails on, a silent guardian of the [[Rustwater Raiders]], with Jackie’s squawks of profanity heralding his every step.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Stoic, restrained, and thunderous when pushed — Cray speaks rarely, but when he does, it’s with the weight of a man who’s already made up his mind.  
**Vices:**  
Violence held in check. Cray is always one breath away from snapping a man in half — and sometimes, that breath’s not worth taking.  
**Abilities:**  
Unmatched in hand-to-hand combat, intimidation, and enforcement; Cray is a walking siege engine who ensures discipline aboard the Black Marauder without ever raising his voice.  
**Secrets:**  
Tucked inside a rusted locket he wears beneath his shirt is a faded photo — a woman and child no one has ever seen. When asked, Cray just walks away.  
**Beliefs:**  
Redemption is earned, not given. Cray believes loyalty is sacred, and the crew that saved him is the only cause worth swinging for.

## Role In Disgraceland
**Blacktide Cray** is the first officer of the _Black Marauder_, Captain Bonetooth’s silent protector and the ship’s living code of consequence. With one eye on the crew and one fist always ready, he doesn’t speak often — but when he moves, _everything listens_.
