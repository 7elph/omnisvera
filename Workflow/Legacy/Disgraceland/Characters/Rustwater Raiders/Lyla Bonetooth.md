## Biography

><h4>“I don’t want your loyalty. I want your fear. Loyalty fades — fear keeps you from turning the wheel.”</h4>

Captain Lyla Bonetooth was born into the turbulent years following the collapse, in the wake of the [[Tetra Bomb]] explosion and the devastating [[water wars]] that followed. The daughter of a fisherman from the [[WATER#Sinners' Sea|Sinner's Sea]] coast, she grew up knowing the unforgiving nature of both the waters and the desperate people who fought over them. Her father, like many others, was conscripted by the government to transport rations and supplies across the newly formed water routes, but when the state’s grip weakened, piracy became the law of the lakes.

Lyla’s rise to power began when she found herself aboard a government watercraft called [[the Black Marauder]]. Initially a supply ship meant to patrol [[Water#Rustwater Bay|Rustwater Bay]], it was poorly guarded and filled with weary soldiers just trying to survive. Sensing the frailty of the crew’s loyalty, Lyla orchestrated a mutiny, leading the ship’s most hardened and bitter members to overthrow their commanding officers. With a blade at the captain's throat, she declared herself the new ruler of the Black Marauder, earning the loyalty of the crew through a mix of ruthlessness and undeniable charisma.

Under her command, the Black Marauder transformed into a dreaded pirate vessel. She navigated the dark waters with uncanny precision, using her cunning to outwit both rival pirate factions and the Duke’s patrols. Her infamy spread quickly — stories of her swift, strategic raids and the brutal punishments dealt to betrayers made her name feared along the coasts. Despite her fierce reputation, those who served her loyally found in Lyla a captain who valued resilience and cunning over brute strength.

A striking figure on the water, Lyla’s dark hair and fair skin contrast sharply with the grime and salt that coat her ship and crew. The eye patch she wears is not simply for intimidation; a skirmish during her early days as captain left her partially blinded. Some whisper it was a mutineer’s last act of defiance, while others say the injury was self-inflicted in a symbolic act to show she feared no pain. Regardless, the patch has become a part of her legend.

Lyla has no patience for weakness or pretense. She rules her crew with an iron resolve, making it clear that every choice comes with consequence. Her word is law aboard the Black Marauder, and while her punishments are swift, she is known to reward loyalty handsomely. The [[Rustwater Raiders]], under her leadership, have become a force to be reckoned with — a relentless tide that sweeps through the bay, plundering resources and leaving only fear in their wake.

## Persoonality
**Temperament:**  
Commanding, merciless, and razor-focused — Lyla doesn’t waste time on second chances. You’re either useful or overboard.

**Vices:**  
Control. She micromanages everything from map routes to punishments, never trusting fate or luck when steel and strategy will do.
 
 **Abilities:**  
Master navigator and naval tactician; unmatched in aquatic guerrilla warfare, mutiny suppression, and psychological intimidation — her name alone can sink a ship’s morale.
 
 **Secrets:**  
Hidden in a locked drawer in her quarters is a bottle of lakewater from the coast where her father died — the only thing she’s kept from her past. She drinks from it once a year. Alone.

**Beliefs:**  
The world is water now — unstable, shifting, and brutal. Lyla believes power belongs to those who seize it, and mercy is a privilege only the strong can afford.

## Role In Disgraceland
**Lyla Bonetooth** is the iron-fanged captain of the _Black Marauder_ and undisputed queen of Rustwater Bay. Pirate, tactician, survivor — her rule is law on the water, and under her, the Rustwater Raiders are more than a fleet. They’re a storm.
