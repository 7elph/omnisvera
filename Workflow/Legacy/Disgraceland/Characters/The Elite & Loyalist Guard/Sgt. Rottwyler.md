## Biography

><h4>“FINGERS OFF!”</h4>

If [[The Duke]] is the mind of Disgraceland, Sgt. Rottwyler is the teeth.

A mountain of a man with arms like tree trunks and a chest built for knocking down doors, Rottwyler is the absolute authority over the [[Loyalist Guard]] — the elite, brutal enforcers who maintain the Duke’s grip on the region. He speaks rarely, growls often, and listens never. His every word sounds like it’s been chewed up with gravel. He is feared more than The Duke himself, and that’s just how he likes it.

Rottwyler was once a mercenary in the Carbine Wastes before the [[Tetra Bomb]] erased most of his former employers. When the smoke cleared, he rode east with nothing but a pack of ammo, a wounded dog, and a pocket full of Slugs. The Duke found him bloodied, half-starved, and still swinging. Instead of killing him, he offered him rank. Rottwyler never forgot that.

His loyalty is absolute. So much so that, in a twisted public ceremony years ago, Rottwyler severed his own left pinky finger as a demonstration of obedience and lifelong service. Now, it’s tradition — every Loyalist must cut off their own pinky to join the Guard. Some faint. Some scream. Rottwyler just watches, cigar clenched between his teeth, eyes cold and unmoved.

That cigar? It’s never gone. No one’s ever seen him without it — even when torturing prisoners or training recruits. It's said he lights it with the ashes of traitors. His breath smells like blood, tobacco, and disdain.

Rottwyler has no family. No past. His body is all scar tissue and routine. Wake. Train. Patrol. Punish. Sleep. He lifts weights in the courtyard of The Silo every dawn, shirtless, while Loyalists stand in formation around him like statues. Dissent is weakness. Mercy is treason. The Duke is law.

But not everyone believes he's unshakable. [[Emily Burton]] once said, in a whisper behind a locked door, “Even dogs break when they’re beaten enough.”

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Unflinching and sadistic — Rottwyler doesn’t speak unless it’s to bark an order, and he enjoys watching fear bloom in a man’s eyes more than he enjoys victory.  
**Vices:**  
Pain — giving it, not feeling it. He trains harder than any recruit, punishes weakness without hesitation, and carries an unholy satisfaction in the breaking of bones and spirits.   
**Abilities:**  
Near-superhuman strength and pain tolerance; master of close-quarters combat and battlefield tactics. His presence alone can quell riots, and his brutality keeps the Loyalist Guard sharp and afraid.  
**Secrets:**  
Beneath his bedframe, wrapped in oilcloth and sealed with wax, is the collar of the dog he rode east with — long dead, buried in Ashmoor's soil. He digs it up every year on the same night, sits with it, and says nothing for hours.  
**Beliefs:**  
The chain of command is sacred. The Duke saved his life, and loyalty to him is the only code that matters. Emotion is for corpses. _“Obey, or be buried.”_

## Role In Disgraceland
Sgt. Rottwyler is the iron fist of The Duke’s regime — commander of the Loyalist Guard and enforcer of obedience through sheer terror. Where The Duke speaks of order, Rottwyler ensures it, one broken jaw at a time.
