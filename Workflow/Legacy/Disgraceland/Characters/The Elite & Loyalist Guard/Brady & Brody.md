## Biography

><h4>“Who you want dead, boss?”</h4>

They don’t talk much. Don’t need to. Just the thud of their boots on tile is enough to make most folks piss themselves.

Brady and Brody — names nobody’s sure they were actually born with — are twin towers of raw, twisted muscle. Always seen together, always looming just a few paces behind The Duke like a matched set of executioners. They’re dressed identically in stained white tank-tops and camouflage pants, belts always tight, fists always clenched. Brady is the bald one with the eye patch and the pin-up girl tattooed on his arm. Brody keeps a flat-top like he’s still pretending to pass inspection. Neither has a neck to speak of.

They are not right, and everyone knows it.

Locals whisper that the twins weren’t born so much as grown, that their freakish size and thick skulls are the legacy of the Tetra Bomb — that their mother died during childbirth and their father was one of the scientists vaporized in the blast. Others claim they were found in a crumbling bunker, half-feral, feeding on rats and old boots. The truth doesn’t matter. What matters is that they are loyal to The Duke to the bone, and they kill without question.

They serve as The Duke’s personal bodyguards, but their duties often stretch into punishment, intimidation, and the occasional public execution. The Duke trots them out during speeches to stand behind him like slabs of meat on legs, staring straight ahead, breathing loud and slow. It’s part of the show. And the people eat it up — or choke on it.

They don’t talk much. But when they do, it’s usually the same thing.

“Who you want dead, boss?”

They're dim, no doubt about that, but not useless. There’s a sort of base cunning in how they move — pack animal instinct. When one starts a fight, the other finishes it. They’ve never been seen apart, never seen sleeping, and no one knows what they do when they’re not standing behind The Duke.

There’s a growing fear that the twins aren’t done changing, that whatever weird residue the Tetra Bomb left in their blood is still brewing. That they might get stronger. Or worse… smarter.

And if that happens?

God help us all.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Silent, violent, and always alert — they don’t speak unless commanded, and they don’t stop once set in motion.
**Vices:**  
Brutality for its own sake. They enjoy breaking bones like others enjoy cracking jokes.
**Abilities:**  
Superhuman strength, uncanny pain resistance, and perfect synchronicity — they fight like a hive mind, overwhelming enemies through sheer force and monstrous stamina.
**Secrets:**  
Beneath Brady’s bunk is a rusted metal lunchbox. Inside? A collection of baby teeth — not their own. No one knows who they belonged to… or why he keeps them.
**Beliefs:**  
The Duke is law. The Duke is god. Their world is black and red — kill or be killed, protect the boss, and crush everything that doesn’t kneel.
