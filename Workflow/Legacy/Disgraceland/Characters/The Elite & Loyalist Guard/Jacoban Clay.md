## Biography

><h4>“Morals don’t build empires. Steel does. And I own the furnace.”</h4>

The wealthiest man in Disgraceland. Few men in Gutter Row rise without getting dirt under their nails. Jacoban Clay didn’t just get dirt — he built an empire from it. The founder of Clay Millworks, Jacoban turned sweat, blood, and iron into wealth. He signed a deal with The Duke early on: free slave labor in exchange for endless shipments of metal — pipes, plates, shackles, weapons. Whatever the regime needed, Jacoban delivered. And he got rich. Fast.

Unlike other industrialists, Jacoban wasn’t loud. He was calm. Calculated. Kept a clean shirt and a loaded pistol. While others begged for permits, Jacoban showed up with a sealed envelope and a nod from The Duke.

Years later, while meeting with officials at The Silo, Jacoban saw a woman enter the room — dark hair pinned back, a red pendant resting just above her breasts, and a look in her eyes like she already owned the place. Her name was Soria Langston. And Jacoban decided then and there: she would be his.

They married shortly after. Now, Jacoban lives lavishly in Ashmoor at her side, managing The Duke’s treasury and watching from behind velvet curtains. He still oversees his factories, still ensures the gears of war turn smoothly. But make no mistake — he didn’t marry Soria for love. He married her because together, they are untouchable. Or so they believe.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Smooth, composed, and opportunistic — Jacoban rarely raises his voice, preferring leverage over force and influence over spectacle.
**Vices:**  
Greed masked as pragmatism. He doesn’t crave attention, only power — and he collects favors, secrets, and leverage like others collect art.
 **Abilities:**  
Gifted industrialist and financial manipulator; he can read contracts like confessionals and has a sixth sense for when to bribe, when to blackmail, and when to vanish a problem.
**Secrets:**  
He keeps a hidden safe behind a false wall in his Ashmoor estate — filled not with money, but with dirt: letters, receipts, and signed orders that could destroy half of The Duke’s inner circle if released.
**Beliefs:**  
Loyalty is a currency, nothing more. In Jacoban’s eyes, survival isn’t about strength — it’s about who controls the flow of iron, gold, and silence.

## Role In Disgraceland
**Jacoban Clay** is the regime’s golden banker — the man behind the curtain who oils the war machine with slave labor and silence. He doesn’t seek the spotlight, but from the luxury of Ashmoor, he ensures the wealth keeps flowing and the truth stays buried.
