## Biography

><h4>“Obey, and endure.”</h4>

His real name is long forgotten — or buried by design. To the people of Disgraceland, he is simply The Duke. A title taken, not earned. He wasn’t elected, appointed, or asked — he declared himself savior in the aftermath of the end of the world. And in a way, he was. Then again, so is a parasite to a corpse.

Before the [[Tetra Bomb]], he had been a high-ranking officer in a private military firm during the final stages of the [[Water Wars]]. Brutal, efficient, and ideologically obsessed with order, he led sieges on fractured city-states and quelled rebellions under the banner of peace. He never blinked at atrocity. His uniform today — a patched-together bastardization of old-world military regalia — is stitched from the flags of defeated enemies, the epaulettes torn from bodies, medals self-assigned.

When the [[Tetra Bomb]] dropped and the world fell silent, The Duke didn’t grieve. He saw opportunity. He took to the woods north of [[WATER#Faded Lake|Faded Lake]] and claimed an old estate once belonging to some long-dead tech mogul. There, he began The Rebuild — a militarized compound that eventually became [[Ashmoor]], with [[The Silo]] at its heart. The Silo is both symbol and weapon: a towering, welded-together grain hoard that feeds the loyal and starves the rest.

The Duke believes that civilization failed because it became too soft. He teaches that the weak destroyed the world, and only by obedience, order, and iron-willed leadership can it be saved. His broadcasts are required viewing across Disgraceland — sermons of fear disguised as hope, always ending with his chilling motto: “Obey, and endure.”

But behind his cold precision lies cruelty. He kidnapped [[Emily Burton]], a child at the time, years ago — the daughter of a dissenting scientist who disappeared soon after. She is now the Duke’s “First Lady”, a forced role inside his mansion-prison. He cannot bear children. He does not touch her. Yet he parades her as a symbol of dominance. The perfect wife. The perfect silence. But not forever.

He hosts his  weekly address, the  [[State of Tribucia]] live every Alphaday on the [[TTV Studios|TTV Network]].

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Controlled and theatrical — he speaks like a preacher but rules like a butcher. He never yells, only issues decrees with unnerving calm and expects the world to obey.

> [!infobox]
>
> ![[prop.png|260]]

**Vices:**  
Delusions of legacy. He feeds on adoration, even if he has to manufacture it. Parades, statues, broadcasts — all scripted to reinforce the fantasy that he _saved_ the world, not conquered its corpse.
**Abilities:**  
Tactical genius in psychological warfare and population control. A master propagandist who builds loyalty through fear, scarcity, and ritual. He can spot dissent in a crowd by a blink.
**Secrets:**  
Deep beneath The Silo, he keeps a sealed chamber filled with relics of the old world — possibly tech, possibly something far darker — and he visits it alone, sometimes for hours, emerging shaken but silent.
 **Beliefs:**  
The strong must rule or all is lost. Emotion is rot. Freedom is a myth. The world didn’t end because of violence — it ended because men stopped _obeying._ His gospel: _“Obey, and endure.”_

## Role In Disgraceland
Self-declared ruler of United Tribucia. Created the militarized territory of **Ashmoor** around an old estate, now known as **The Silo**. Commands the **Loyalist Guard**, controls food distribution, and uses propaganda to keep control. Directly responsible for multiple atrocities across the land, all committed under the guise of “the greater good.” Hosts daily propaganda announcements across all of United Tribucia every night at 18:00 hrs. Mandatory viewing.
