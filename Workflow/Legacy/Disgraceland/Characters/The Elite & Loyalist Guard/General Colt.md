## Biography

>><h4>“Obedience is efficient. Loyalty is inefficient. I reward efficiency.”</h4>

If Sgt. Rottwyler is The Duke’s hammer, General Colt is his scalpel. 

Colt was born in a sealed military bunker somewhere deep in the Upper Peninsula during the final years of the [[Water Wars]]. The son of officers, he was raised on cold meals, colder lectures, and war manuals instead of bedtime stories. 

When the [[Tetra Bomb]] dropped, he was one of the few with enough tactical foresight to see which way the power was shifting — and moved quickly to align himself with the rising Duke. He doesn’t believe in loyalty. He believes in utility. While Sgt. Rottwyler commands the troops through fear and brute dominance, Colt oversees operations, strategy, and propaganda. He coordinates fuel shipments, monitors dissenters, and signs off on disappearances. 

Nothing happens without his mark on it — from the placement of radio towers to which town gets its power back first after an outage. 

[[The Duke]] trusts him. Or at least needs him. Colt knows this — and uses it. There is no warmth in the man. He never married. Never took a lover. His life is a ledger of useful actions and eliminated threats. He believes The Duke’s vision for [[DISGRACELAND]] is flawed, but necessary. He sees himself as a stabilizer — keeping the regime from crumbling under the weight of its own absurdity. 

He finds Rottwyler repugnant — a snarling dog too eager to punish when a whisper could silence more effectively. The two men hate each other, but both know a war within the Guard would burn them both. So they maintain a quiet truce, marked by veiled threats and stiff nods in the war room. Colt is never without his gloves, a sharp uniform, and a plan. 

He keeps stacks of black notebooks in his quarters, filled with observations, patterns, and projections. Rumors say he knows every possible outcome of a rebellion, including what to do if The Duke himself dies. The only thing he fears… is chaos. And deep down, he knows: chaos is coming.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Precise, clinical, and coldly analytical — Colt doesn’t act on emotion, only on probability and outcome.  

> [!infobox]
>
> ![[coltwalk.png|260]]

 **Vices:**  
 Control through information. He hoards data, monitors everyone, and plays puppet master with towns, people, and perception like pieces on a war board.  
 **Abilities:**  
 Master strategist and tactician with an unmatched ability to predict political unrest, manipulate public sentiment, and suppress rebellion before it ignites.  
 **Secrets:**  
 Buried in his ledgers is a full contingency plan detailing how to dissolve The Duke’s regime from within — and replace it with something leaner, smarter, and far more terrifying.  
 **Beliefs:**  
 Order is survival. The Duke is flawed, but necessary… for now. Sentiment is a liability, and mercy is a luxury only victors can afford.

## Role In Disgraceland
**General Colt** is the unseen architect behind Disgraceland’s machinery — controlling supply lines, information flow, and political stability with ruthless efficiency. While The Duke preaches obedience and Rottwyler enforces it, Colt ensures the entire system doesn’t collapse under its own brutality.
