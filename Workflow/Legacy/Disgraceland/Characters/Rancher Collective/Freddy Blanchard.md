## Biography

><h4>"I don't need a reason to feed the hungry. I need a reason not to burn this whole goddamn deal down."</h4>

They say Freddy Blanchard was born in a storm — his mother died giving birth, and his father died trying to raise him. He grew up dirt-covered and half-starved, shoveling coal and breaking wild horses for pennies in the cracked shadow of Charspire. By the time he was fifteen, Freddy could lift more than a grown man and take a punch that’d kill one too. But he wasn’t just muscle — he had purpose.

He earned every acre of Bonebreak Ranch with blood and grit. When the water wars came, and the bombs fell, Freddy didn’t run or loot. He planted. Dug wells. Slaughtered meat. Built shelter. Folks came to him starving and hopeless — he fed ‘em, no questions asked.  
The Duke calls it charity. Freddy calls it survival.

Bonebreak Ranch is the largest in Threshton, and Freddy works every inch of it with his own damn hands. The other ranchers got foremen and hired guns — Freddy’s up at dawn slinging hay bales and breaking fence with a rusted hammer and a stronger will. His back is covered in scars, his hands thick with callus, but his people swear they’ve never gone hungry under his watch.

Underneath that gruff, stoic jawline and cannonball chest is a fire — a deep hatred for the Duke’s taxes, rations, and cruelty. Freddy’s the reason whole families are still alive, thanks to backdoor cattle runs and secret crop shipments smuggled through Gutter Row by moonlight. He’s made deals with pirates, bartered with outcasts, and strongarmed corrupt checkpoint guards, all without taking a single coin for himself.

The other ranchers respect him like a lion — Turner Anderson may be the glue of the Collective, but Freddy’s the iron nail. And when the fighting finally comes, folks say Blanchard’ll be the one leading the charge, shovel in one hand, Duke’s blood in the other.

## Personality

> [!NOTE|clean no-i right]+ ‎Rancher Meeting
> ![[bf1.png|400]]  

**Temperament:**  
Gruff and intense, but fiercely loyal. Freddy doesn’t speak unless it’s worth saying, and when he does, it lands like a punch. He leads by example, not instruction.

**Vices:**  
Sleeps in the barn more than his own bed. Carries guilt like a second skin. Hates being idle — work is his only medicine.

**Abilities:**  
Physically powerful, borderline unstoppable in a fight.  Skilled rancher, hunter, and makeshift engineer.  Unshakable willpower; doesn’t fear pain, loss, or the Duke.  Tactical thinker when it comes to 
smuggling and logistics.

**Secrets:**  
Keeps a hidden stash of weapons buried under the north field in case war breaks out.  Still communicates with someone inside Gutter Row — likely one of the pirate clans.  Had a brother who joined the Loyalist Guard and was later executed by the Duke for defiance.

**Beliefs:**  
No man should go hungry.  The land belongs to those who work it.  The Duke's rule is built on fear, and fear burns like anything else.

## Role In Disgraceland
Freddy Blanchard is the enforcer spirit of the Rancher Collective — the man who does what others won’t, to protect what matters most. While Turner Anderson balances the ledger and charts the course, Freddy keeps the wolves at bay and the people fed by any means necessary. His silent rebellion feeds families from the shadows, and if the time for open war comes, he won’t wait for permission — he’ll lead the first charge himself.
