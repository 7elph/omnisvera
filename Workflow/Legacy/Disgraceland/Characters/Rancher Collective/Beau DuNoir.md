## Biography

><h4>"Life's just a crooked hand of cards, darlin'. I don't cheat. I just play smarter."</h4>

One day, few years back, Beau found himself seated at the infamous Red Widow table — a high-stakes game hosted by a dying cattle baron named **Olin Vex**, too proud to admit his empire was crumbling under the weight of his addictions. Beau, ever the opportunist, kept his tells hidden behind gold-rimmed glasses and a honeyed drawl. When the dust settled, Vex was broke, crying, and dead of a heart attack. Beau walked out with the deed to [[Hangman's Ranch]] in one hand and a glass of Widow’s Whiskey in the other.

Since then, Beau’s made Hangman’s Ranch into a place of mystique. It ain't the biggest spread in Threshton, but it *feels* like it is. The saloon girls drift in and out of his halls, and the locals whisper tales about what really happens in his smoke-filled parlor. Nobody knows how he keeps his cattle so well-fed while other ranchers starve — some say he’s got deals with the pirates out of [[Rustwater Bay]], or even with [[The Duke’s]] men. Others say he cheats death at the same table he cheated Vex.

Beau dresses like a king, even if his boots are scuffed with blood. Emerald coats, ruffled shirts, rings on every finger — it’s all part of the performance. He likes being seen, likes the spotlight. But he’s got that killer instinct under the charm. The man smiles when he talks, even if he’s promising to burn your barn down.

The [[Rancher Collective]] sees him as a necessary evil — valuable when negotiating, dangerous when crossed. [[Turner Anderson]] trusts him about as far as he can spit, but damn if Beau ain’t handy at turning the odds. He'll fight if he has to, but he’d rather win with cards or charm. And if he does pull a blade… you won’t see it coming.

## Personality

**Temperament:**  
Flamboyant and silver-tongued. Loves theatrics, hates boredom, always five steps ahead. Never lets you see the real hand he’s playing.

**Vices:**  
Whiskey, games of chance, beautiful trouble. Lies like he breathes — effortlessly and often.

**Abilities:**  
Master manipulator and world-class gambler.  Excellent negotiator and speaker — can talk a guard into dropping his rifle.  Skilled with hidden blades and sleight of hand.  Knows how to move goods (and secrets) through dangerous territory.

**Secrets:**  
Keeps coded ledgers of every secret deal he's made — including one with a Loyalist Guard captain.  Still corresponds with a widow in Gutter Row whose son he once saved — and may have fathered.  Believes the Duke’s downfall will be economic, not bloody — and is already moving to profit from it.

**Beliefs:**  
Chaos is a ladder, and he intends to climb.  The gods are gamblers, and he’s their favorite piece.  Trust is just another currency — and it's always inflating.

## Role In Disgraceland
Beau DuNoir is the wildcard of the Rancher Collective — the man who wins wars before the shooting starts. He’s the negotiator, the wheeler-dealer, the man you send when you need a dirty job cleaned up with style. While Turner builds, and Freddy protects, Beau ensures Threshton stays just slippery enough to survive. He’s a snake in a velvet coat — and sometimes, that’s exactly what you need.
