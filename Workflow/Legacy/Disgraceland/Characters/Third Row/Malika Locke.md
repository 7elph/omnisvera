## Biography

> “He talks, I cut. That’s balance.”

**Malika Locke** walks through [[Gutter Row]] like she owns every shadow. Known as the ride-or-die of [[Ninepack]], she’s more than his girl — she’s his backup plan, his bodyguard, his bruiser, and sometimes, his conscience. Born and raised in the broken back alleys of Vileby, Malika earned her name the hard way — beating older boys in dice games and outrunning Loyalist patrols by the time she was ten.

She first met Ninepack during a chaotic street deal that went sideways. While others ran, she stepped in and bailed him out with a knife to someone’s throat and a smirk on her face. He talked slick. She didn’t care. But over time, their bond solidified — not just built on love, but on blood, danger, and a shared need to stay one move ahead of the streets.

Malika doesn’t say much in public, but her presence speaks volumes. She’s known for carrying two blades, moving like smoke through the Three Nail Market, and finishing fights her man never had to start. The only person she listens to is Ninepack, and even that depends on the mood.

She’s a legend in the making, feared by rivals and whispered about by Market kids who dream of being half as bad.

## Personality

**Temperament:** 
Calm until it’s time to strike. Observant, fast-thinking, loyal to a razor edge.

**Vices:** 
Dice games, fire whiskey, and putting men in their place. Keeps her late mother’s ring on a chain around her neck.

**Abilities:** 
Close-quarters combat, sleight of hand, stealth, tracking. Sharp instincts — she spots trouble before it speaks.

**Secrets:** 
Her real last name is **Vorneaux**, tying her to a disgraced Loyalist bloodline. She’s buried the past deep, but it has claws.

**Beliefs:** 
Trust no one except Ninepack. Loyalty is everything. And survival without love ain’t worth the scars.

## Role in Disgraceland
Malika Locke is the quiet fire beside Ninepack’s silver tongue — a lethal protector who watches his back and burns anyone who comes too close.
