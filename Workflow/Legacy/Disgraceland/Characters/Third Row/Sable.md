## Biography

> “I don’t need to raise my voice. The room already belongs to me.”

**Sable Marris** grew up on the _rich edge of ruin_ — the daughter of a failed merchant who taught her two things before skipping town: how to count money, and how to count men. She learned to fend for herself early, moving through [[Vileby]]’s backdoor lounges and dust-shack casinos with poise and heat, using words as weapons and silk as armor. But she was never just chasing a man — she was chasing a throne.

Then came **Samson Knight**. She didn’t beg for his attention. She caught it. And held it. What started as mutual amusement turned to something more dangerous: trust.

Sable is the one woman who can speak plainly to Mr. Knight without drawing fire. She doesn’t walk behind him — she walks beside him, dressed to kill and twice as sharp. While the world sees her as a trophy of his empire, those who know better realize: she helps shape it.

Behind the scenes, Sable acts as a silent advisor, an event planner, and a social manipulator. She knows how to read power structures like blueprints. She knows who to bribe, who to blackmail, and when to disappear. She doesn’t pull the trigger, but she picks the target.

## Personality

**Temperament:** 
Graceful, deliberate, and iron-willed. Never loud, always heard.

**Vices:** 
Jewelry, red wine, slow jazz, and perfectly timed revenge.

**Abilities:** 
Influence, information gathering, manipulation. Knows how to break men using words alone.

**Secrets:** 
Keeps detailed records on every deal Knight has ever made — and a backup copy hidden outside the city.

**Beliefs:** 
Power is more beautiful when it’s quiet. Comfort is earned. And no man rules alone.

## Role in Disgraceland
Sable Marris is the unspoken empress of Samson Knight’s world — the velvet whisper behind his iron presence, helping him rule Gutter Row with subtlety, glamour, and cold precision.
