## Biography

> "I ain’t here to talk. I’m here to remember.”

Born into the gutter, raised by fists, and rebuilt in violence, **Jailface** is the scarred iron-fanged lieutenant of The [[Third Row]] and the personal enforcer of [[Samson Knight]]. His true name has been lost — maybe forgotten, maybe discarded — but his new name was earned the day a rival gang welded a prison brace to his skull and left him for dead in the ash canals. He survived. He came back. He didn’t speak a word. Just started cutting.

Jailface grew up just two blocks from Row Three, a street kid with nothing to lose and too much anger to bury. In the early days of the Row Wars, he worked underground pit fights to feed himself — one fight cost him most of his teeth. The next gave him a reputation. By the time the old guard of The Third Row had fallen apart, Jailface was a half-legend in Gutter Row already, known for finishing what others wouldn’t start.

When [[Samson Knight]] began consolidating power in the 2170s, Jailface didn’t challenge him. He offered him something else — silence, skill, and a debt of loyalty. Some say Samson was the one who found him after the brace incident, pulled him out of the filth, and gave him purpose. Since then, Jailface has been at Knight’s right side: a shadow in the alleys, a whisper before the blood hits the floor.

No one speaks directly to Jailface unless they’re invited. And no one runs in the [[Three Nail Market]] without his approval.

## Personality

**Temperament:** 
Unflinching, cold-blooded, and eerily quiet. Most of the time, he lets silence do the talking — until it’s time to scream.

**Vices:** 
**Burn Root** — a black, mind-numbing combat stimulant he chews constantly. That, and the slow act of revenge.

**Abilities:** 
Close-quarters killing, brutal enforcement, knife and machete combat, fear management. Exceptionally loyal, calculating when needed.

**Secrets:** 
The iron brace wasn’t just punishment — it was betrayal. Some whisper it was an inside job by the old gang leadership. Jailface keeps a kill list tattooed along his ribs. Many of the names are already crossed out.

**Beliefs:** 
Knight is king. Orders are scripture. Anyone who touches the market without permission gets touched back — harder.

## Role in Disgraceland
Jailface is the bloody teeth of [[Samson Knight]]’s rule — a walking warning to every hustler, traitor, and outsider in Gutter Row. He enforces the market’s invisible code and ensures fear is never forgotten.
