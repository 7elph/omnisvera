## Biography

> “If you step into my market, you play by my math. You don’t like the numbers? Don’t come back.”


**Samson Knight**, known across Gutter Row as **Mr. Knight**, is the commanding leader of [[Third Row]] — the dominant street gang that controls the heart of the [[Three Nail Market]] in [[Vileby]]. A towering, broad-shouldered Black man with a clipped beard and eyes that don’t blink when they should, Samson carries the aura of someone who’s seen the worst and decided to become worse.

Born in a crumbling tenement on Row Three sometime in the 2150s, Samson was raised amid violence, ration lines, and betrayal. His mother worked market tables, his father disappeared during the Charspire riots, and by thirteen Samson was already known as a quiet enforcer who didn’t need to yell to make you fold. He climbed the ranks of the gang as muscle, but when the founders fell — Eddie to a back-alley bullet and Shad to the chaos of the [[Blackshift Massacre]] — he didn’t grieve. He moved.

In the fractured years that followed, when The Third Row splintered into infighting and ruin, Samson played a long game. He killed who he had to. Paid who he couldn’t. And whispered unity until it became law. By 2178, there was no one left to challenge him — not in the gang, not in the market.

Today, Mr. Knight runs the Three Nail Market like a quiet king. He doesn’t yell, doesn’t flinch, and doesn’t forget. He counts slugs by the firelight in the backrooms of bloodstained buildings, surrounded by killers who owe him their lives — or fear losing them.

## Personality

**Temperament:** 
Calm, calculating, immovable; speaks slowly but hits fast. Keeps control through presence, not theatrics.

**Vices:** 
Money, memory, and power — and maybe a taste for control that goes deeper than business.

**Abilities:** 
Expert at organization, intimidation, and maintaining order through layered fear. Brilliant strategist with unmatched street knowledge of Gutter Row.

**Secrets:** 
Keeps a black-bound book of names — debts owed, promises broken, and things that can't be forgiven. Some say it includes [[The Duke of Disgraceland]].

**Beliefs:** 
Loyalty is earned in blood. Money is the only true voice in Disgraceland. The past is a weapon — sharpen it.

## Role in Disgraceland
Samson Knight is the silent king of the streets, holding together the underworld economy of Gutter Row while ensuring the Three Nail Market stays open, profitable, and absolutely his.
