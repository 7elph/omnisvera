## Biography

><h4>“Loyalty is a matter of leverage. Nothing more.”</h4>

The only daughter of Threshton's oldest and most revered rancher, [[Harp Langston]], Soria was raised amid grit, cattle, and the stubborn code of rancher pride. But she was never interested in crops or community. She wanted influence — power wrapped in silk, not soil.

Years ago, when whispers of rebellion stirred in the fields and ranchers began secretly feeding starving citizens, Soria made a choice. One night, she slipped away and told a group of Loyalist Guards exactly who was involved.

It was betrayal. Of blood and home. And it paid off.

[[The Duke]], ever hungry for loyalty and leverage, brought her to [[The Silo]]. Soria’s sharp tongue and sharper mind caught his eye. She became his scribe, organizing his affairs, drafting his decrees. Some say she seduced him. Others say she simply outmaneuvered every other vulture in the room.

When [[Ashmoor]]’s previous mayor drowned in his own bath under “peculiar circumstances,” The Duke didn’t hesitate. He appointed Soria as mayor — elegant, merciless, and loyal only to herself.

She now presides over the wealthiest, most reconstructed part of [[United Tribucia]], where she hosts opulent galas while the rest of the land starves. Her citizens loathe her. The Duke trusts her. And her father — if he's still alive — likely wishes he had strangled her in the crib.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Charming, cunning, and quietly venomous. Always composed — never shows anger, only disapproval.
**Vices:**  
Control, vanity, and the thrill of betrayal. Seduction is both a tool and a weapon.
**Abilities:**  
Political maneuvering, manipulation, and flawless rhetoric. She can dismantle a room without raising her voice — or lifting a finger.
**Secrets:**  
Maintains a secret ledger of every bribe, betrayal, and favor owed inside The Silo — including dirt on The Duke himself. She’s preparing for something bigger.
**Beliefs:**  
Power is beauty. Loyalty is transactional. Weakness is a sin. Everyone is replaceable — even blood.

## Role in Disgraceland
[[Soria Langston-Clay]] is the ruthless architect of Ashmoor’s political machine. Though she owes her rise to The Duke, her ambitions stretch beyond mere servitude. She embodies everything her father despised — a traitor cloaked in velvet. Her enemies call her poison in perfume. Her allies know better than to call her anything at all.
