## Biography

><h4>“What’s mine is mine. And what’s yours... is mine too, if I want it.”</h4>

The farmland of [[Threshton]] was supposed to be the breadbasket of United Tribucia—rolling crops, fat cattle, and grain enough to feed a thousand. Instead, it's a graveyard of hunger, and at the top of the food chain sits Mayor [[Marla Rusk]], bloated and smug, gnawing on roasted drumsticks while her people scrape mold off potato skins.

Rusk wasn’t elected. Hell, she wasn’t even picked. She offered herself up to [[The Duke]] as a loyal pair of eyes and ears—specifically aimed at the [[Rancher Collective]]. She promised to keep them in line, expose secret trades, and sniff out disloyalty like a truffle pig. In return, she demanded two things: immunity from oversight, and the right to “reclaim” any food she found.

And oh, did she find food.

Rusk hoards it all—grain, meat, fresh fruit, pickled goods, root cellars stacked to the rafters in the mayor’s estate while the rest of [[Threshton]] suffers under her rule. Her slaves, the largest number in all of DISGRACELAND, are forced to work her private farms with nothing but thin broth and beatings for fuel. Anyone who questions her gets shackled or disappears into the cornfields.

She rules with greed and gossip.

No one trusts her, not even The Duke’s other mayors. But she doesn’t need trust. She’s useful. She watches the ranchers—especially [[Turner Anderson]] and [[Freddy Blanchard]], who she resents deeply. She keeps a ledger of what goes in and out of every farm, tracking everything like a bloated tax hawk. If someone’s feeding citizens under the table, Rusk finds out.

Her mansion, the Gullet House, is a grotesque palace of overindulgence. Roasting pits run day and night. Servants fan her while she eats. Pigs are fattened, then killed for entertainment. She throws Feast Nights where Loyalist guests watch slaves fight for scraps while Rusk laughs with a turkey leg in each hand.

But the land remembers.  
And so do the people.  
And if hunger doesn’t kill her, rage might.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Boastful, petty, and dangerously paranoid. Quick to punish, quicker to indulge.
**Vices:**  
Gluttony, greed, voyeurism. Addicted to power and full plates.
**Abilities:**  
Obsessive recordkeeping, cruel manipulation, political blackmail. She knows what everyone eats and who fed them.
**Secrets:**  
Keeps secret trade records on every rancher, including past betrayals by [[Soria Langston-Clay]]. May have poisoned a Rancher Collective member years ago to earn her spot.
**Beliefs:**  
Hunger is control. Everything can be owned. Generosity is treason.

## Role in Disgraceland
[[Marla Rusk]] rules [[Threshton]] with a stomach full of stolen grain and an ego too fat to fail. Her value to The Duke lies in her ability to track every seed and slaughter—no matter how small. But her reign isn’t built to last. If the people ever rise, she’ll be the first to fall.
