## Biography

><h4>“They don’t have to love me. Just fear me enough not to act.”</h4>

Once a union-buster, always a traitor. Ellis Westmount was a corporate enforcer before the bombs ever dropped—a man who made his fortune breaking the backs of blue-collar workers while pretending to protect them. When the men and women of Steeltown tried to reclaim their future by unionizing the steel plants, Westmount sold them out to the Loyalist Guard before the ink was even dry on their demands.

The Duke rewarded him with the mayorship like a dog gets a bone for baring its teeth. Now Ellis runs Steeltown from behind iron walls and the barrels of Loyalist rifles. He wears a three-piece suit like he’s above the soot, but the people know better.

He’s hated. Not disliked. Not distrusted. Hated.

He can’t walk through Steeltown without a wall of bodyguards. Most days, he doesn’t try. He rules through surveillance, propaganda, and brute force. The factories run day and night, and anyone caught whispering the word “strike” ends up in the Blacksmoke Tunnels beneath the plant—if they’re lucky.

But Ellis doesn’t care if they like him. He cares about results. About being feared. And in a town built on sweat and fire, fear is the only currency he trusts.  
  
The former mayor was killed in a riot following the death of a Wrecking Crew member, and deep down Westmount knows he’s sharing power in this city with the Wrecking Crew whether he likes it or not.**


## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Ruthless, calculating, and cold. Never raises his voice—he doesn't need to. Every word is a threat.
**Vices:**  
Power addiction, sadism, and a deep contempt for the working class. Drinks privately but never shows weakness in public.
**Abilities:**  
Master manipulator and tactician. Expert in surveillance, psychological warfare, and crushing dissent before it takes root. He doesn’t fight himself—but he always knows who will.
**Secrets:**  
Still receives direct orders from The Duke through an encrypted comms line—something even the Loyalist Guard captains aren’t aware of. Also keeps files on every Wrecking Crew member, waiting for the moment he can move against them.
**Beliefs:**  
Order is worth more than freedom. Fear keeps people alive. Loyalty is earned through domination, not trust.

## Role in Disgraceland
Ellis Westmount rules [[Steeltown]] with an iron grip, installed by [[The Duke]] after selling out the city’s workers during the last days of the steel unions. Though he holds the mayor’s title, real power is contested—[[Steeltown Wrecking Crew]] looms large, and Westmount knows any wrong move could turn the city against him overnight. He’s the face of oppression in a city built on rebellion.
