## Biography

><h4>“Hope don’t run a furnace. Fear does.”</h4>

In the smog-strangled hellscape of [[Charspire]], where molten runoff glows in the gutters and the air tastes like burning metal, Mayor [[Gorren Brile]] sits fat and content atop a mountain of suffering he helped build.

He didn’t rise to power through merit. He slithered his way into it—feeding [[The Duke]] a steady diet of intelligence pulled from the slums, the mills, and the whispers of the desperate. For years, Brile was a name no one said out loud, just a shadow that passed coin for secrets and disappeared before the screaming started.

When the old foreman-mayor “vanished” during a boiler explosion, Brile was already waiting in a fresh suit with a new title.

Now, as Duke-appointed mayor, he oversees [[Charspire]] with bloated arrogance, a snake-headed cane in one hand and a smoldering cigar in the other. He keeps the mines running, the mills churning, and the death toll climbing.

But it’s not Brile himself that people fear.  
It’s his [[RedForce]].

A handpicked squad of bruisers, burnouts, and sadists in blood-red armor—the RedForce are more gang than guard. Bankrolled by the Loyalist Guard, they wear dark leather dusters with crimson plates over their chests, and helmets shaped like steel-welded frowns. Their job is simple: enforce, extract, erase.

They drag "disloyal" workers into the streets and make examples of them. They storm homes in the middle of the night. They torture rebels in boiler rooms and chain corpses to smokestacks as warnings. The RedForce doesn’t arrest. They punish.

Brile watches it all from his brass office above the slagfields—pockets lined, trousers stained, and ego swollen. He makes regular visits to [[GUTTER ROW]], always under heavy escort, to indulge in the red-light vices with sickening pride. His appetites are legendary, and his reputation just as filthy.

No one loves Mayor Gorren Brile.  
But no one dares remove him either.  
Not yet.  
Because in Charspire, fear still outvotes hope.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Lecherous, loud, and quick to anger. Treats fear like applause.
**Vices:**  
Sex, violence, gluttony, cruelty. Keeps a collection of gold rings he’s taken off corpses.
**Abilities:**  
Bribery, blackmail, and brutal intimidation. Doesn’t need to be smart—just needs to know who to break.
**Secrets:**  
Keeps a hidden tunnel from his office to a Gutter Row brothel. Maintains secret recordings of Duke meetings for leverage. Claims to have dirt on every other mayor.
**Beliefs:**  
Fear gets results. Loyalty is a lie people tell to stay alive. The strong survive, the useful thrive, and the rest burn.

## Role in Disgraceland
[[Gorren Brile]] rules [[Charspire]] with bloated pride and violent indulgence, relying on the infamous [[RedForce]] to crush opposition before it can breathe. He is everything wrong with the industrial world made flesh—a greedy pig wrapped in silk and soot. But The Duke needs his brutality, and the factories need to run, so Brile stays in power… for now.
