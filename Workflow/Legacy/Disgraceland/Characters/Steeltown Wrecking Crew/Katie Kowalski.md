## Biography

><h4>“I don’t wear this bandana for fashion. I wear it to remind people that someone still gives a damn whether they live.”</h4>

**Katie Kowalski** is the heartbeat of the Wrecking Crew — a healer in a world that chews up the kind and spits out the broken. But Katie isn’t soft. She’s iron-willed, fire-hearted, and sharper than any blade.

Raised in the ruins of Steeltown to a nurse and a machinist, Katie taught herself medicine with scavenged books, stories, and stitched-together anatomy charts. Now she keeps the Crew alive with bandages, tinctures, and sheer determination.

She met [[Mark Jeffrey]] and [[Joe Ameen]] in their wild youth — and never left their side. Her bond with Mark runs deep, full of unsaid truths and dangerous affection. Joe’s laughter helps her breathe through the darkness. She would die for both… but she prefers to keep them alive.

Katie doesn’t carry a gun. She carries the will to save people who’ve been forgotten. Her bandana, gifted by Mark, is her armor — a symbol of mercy in a place where mercy is rare. And if you think that makes her weak, you’ve never watched her rebuild a body with nothing but grit, tape, and bloody hands.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Compassionate, focused, and calm under pressure. Katie doesn’t raise her voice — she just starts patching the wounds.

**Vices:**  
Self-sacrifice. She’ll bleed herself dry before she lets someone else fall.
**Abilities:**  
Self-taught combat medic with expert trauma care and battlefield strategy. A field general in scrubs.
**Secrets:**  
A worn letter from Joe Ameen lives hidden in her pack. She’s never told him she found it. But she keeps it. Always.
**Beliefs:**  
Saving a life is an act of rebellion in Disgraceland. Katie fights for every pulse, because every breath is defiance.

## Role In Disgraceland
**Katie Kowalski** is the Wrecking Crew’s lifeline. Their doctor, their conscience, their firebrand of hope. She doesn’t wear armor — she makes sure the rest of them survive to wear it.
