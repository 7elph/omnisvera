## Biography

><h4>“I ain’t a hero. I’m just the guy who makes sure the hero gets to stand back up.”</h4>

**Abel May Jr.** is the wall you hit when you run out of bad ideas. Towering, scarred, and sharp-eyed, he’s the Crew’s sledgehammer — the one who doesn’t swing unless it counts, and when it counts, nothing gets back up.

Years ago, Jimmy and Mark found him bleeding out in the mud after a failed Duke ambush. They saved him. He’s been saving them right back ever since.

Now Abel walks the line between warrior and anchor. He speaks few words. Lets his fists say the rest. But around a fire, drink in hand, he’ll sing songs that could level hearts — about family, loss, and things broken long ago.

His cousins, [[Brady & Brody]] Grody, are monsters in service to the Duke. Abel calls them “the pet freaks” — but every year, he writes them a letter. Unsent. Unopened. He doesn’t know if he wants to forgive them or put them down.

He fights for the Crew like they’re blood. Because for him, they are. And if Jimmy’s the rebel’s flame, and Mark the brain, Abel’s the concrete — unshakable, unmovable, and still standing when everyone else falls.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Silent, strong, and deadly — Abel leads with presence, not volume. Calm until provoked. Then the earth shakes.
**Vices:**  
Whiskey and sorrow songs — he drinks to forget, and sings to remember.
**Abilities:**  
One-man wrecking crew. His fists hit like warhammers, and his presence alone clears rooms.
**Secrets:**  
A locked ammo box under his bunk contains years of unsent letters to his cousins. Even monsters leave shadows behind.
**Beliefs:**  
Family is chosen. And if you stand with the Crew, he’ll bury whoever tries to bring you down — even if they share his blood.

## Role In Disgraceland
**Abel May Jr.** is the wrecking part of the Wrecking Crew — the Crew’s immovable defender, bruiser, and bruised soul. He may not speak often, but when he does, people shut up and listen.
