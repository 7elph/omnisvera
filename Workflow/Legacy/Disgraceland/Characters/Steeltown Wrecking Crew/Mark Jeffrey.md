## Biography

><h4>“You better pray to El Santo.”</h4>

If [[Jimmy Douglass]] is the soul of the Wrecking Crew, **Mark Jeffrey** is its brain — the tactician with a cigarette in his teeth and a smirk that means trouble. While Jimmy charges through the fire, Mark calculates the exits. Together, they’re unstoppable.

Raised in the industrial grind of Steeltown, Mark grew up watching his brother fight to protect their people. But while Jimmy inherited their father’s brawler spirit, Mark leaned into the long game. He studies people, studies maps, and makes sure the Crew doesn’t rush into a fight it can’t finish. But don’t mistake planning for cowardice — Mark’s fists speak fluent rebellion, and his knuckles know the Duke’s loyalists by name.

He’s got a crooked smile, a scar near his collarbone, and a laugh that defuses tension faster than any weapon. His tactical brilliance keeps the Crew on course — but it’s his sense of humor and loyalty that keep them from falling apart.

Mark may never seek the spotlight, but without him, the whole movement would collapse under its own fire. He’s the anchor behind Jimmy’s spark — and the one who’ll keep Disgraceland standing once the smoke clears.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Clever, calm, and always in control — Mark’s the Crew’s most stable hand, equally quick with a plan or a joke.
**Vices:**  
Chain-smoker, deep thinker. Covers his fears with sarcasm and secondhand smoke.
**Abilities:**  
Master strategist, intuitive mediator, and gifted with people — he diffuses fights, predicts enemy moves, and plans logistics like a general.
**Secrets:**  
A hidden notebook under his mattress contains his blueprint for rebuilding Disgraceland after the Duke. No one’s seen it. Not even Jimmy.
**Beliefs:**  
Revolutions are only worth fighting if you’ve got something better to build when the dust settles. Mark’s here to win the future, not just the war.
