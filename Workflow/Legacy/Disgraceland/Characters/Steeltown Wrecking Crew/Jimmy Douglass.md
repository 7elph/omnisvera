## Biography

><h4>“Whose streets? Our streets!”</h4>

Jimmy Douglass was born and raised in the rusted shadows of Steeltown, a once-thriving industrial hub before the Water wars and the subsequent [[Tetra Bomb]]. His father, [[Darrell Douglass]], was a stubborn steelworker who believed in loyalty, brotherhood, and doing what’s right — values Jimmy carries to this day. After Darrell’s death during the early chaos of Disgraceland’s formation, Jimmy and his younger brother, [[Mark Jeffrey]], vowed to honor his legacy. They founded the [[Steeltown Wrecking Crew]] as a sanctuary for those cast aside by the Duke’s ruthless regime.

Tall and broad-shouldered, with silver-streaked hair and a weathered beard, Jimmy commands respect. His body tells the story of his battles — a patchwork of scars beneath tattoos that chronicle both his losses and victories. His "[[Hijos Del Darrell]]" shirt is more than a uniform; it’s a tribute to the man who taught him strength and resilience.

Despite his gruff exterior and the ever-present cigarette between his fingers, Jimmy possesses a sharp mind. He is a strategist, always planning the next move. Some say he’s reckless, pushing the Crew closer to open rebellion with every confrontation. But others see him as a symbol of resistance — a man who refuses to bow.

Jimmy loathes [[The Duke]] of Disgraceland and everything he stands for. He views the Duke’s rule as a parasite feeding on the suffering of the people. While some factions seek power for themselves, Jimmy has no interest in crowns or thrones. His fight is for freedom, not control.

However, he knows war is not just fought with weapons. The Steeltown Wrecking Crew has become a beacon of hope, providing food, shelter, and protection to the oppressed. Jimmy has established underground schools for children and organized aid for families left destitute by the Duke’s brutal taxes. In doing so, he has become a hero to the people — a leader they willingly follow.

Yet, beneath the strength and resolve lies a man burdened by loss. The choices he’s made weigh heavily on him. Every member of the Wrecking Crew lost someone, and Jimmy carries those ghosts. His younger brother, Mark Jeffrey, is his anchor. While their bond is unbreakable, Mark’s cautious nature often puts him at odds with Jimmy’s more aggressive tactics.

But for Jimmy, there is no middle ground. The Duke’s reign must end, no matter the cost. He believes the people of Disgraceland are ready to rise — and when they do, the Steeltown Wrecking Crew will be leading the charge.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Fierce, driven, and uncompromising — Jimmy leads with his gut and doesn’t flinch when the cost of doing what’s right is blood.
**Vices:**  
Stubborn pride and chain-smoking — he burns through both with equal intensity, refusing to slow down or back down even when the smarter play is patience.
**Abilities:**  
Gifted battlefield commander and natural rallying point — his presence inspires unity, and his speeches have lit more fires than any torch or match.
**Secrets:**  
In the bottom drawer of his desk sits a faded photo of his father and a sealed envelope labeled “For Mark.” He hasn’t opened it. Not in ten years. He’s afraid of what it says — or what it asks of him.    
**Beliefs:**  
Justice is non-negotiable. The Duke is a disease, and Disgraceland won’t heal until he’s ripped out root and stem. Jimmy believes in the people — and that they’re worth every scar.

## Role In Disgraceland
**Jimmy Douglass** is the rebel soul of Disgraceland — founder and president of the Steeltown Wrecking Crew, a man who turned grief into purpose. His mission isn’t power. It’s liberation. And when the final reckoning comes, Jimmy won’t be watching from the sidelines — he’ll be leading the charge with a cigarette in his mouth and a war cry on his lips.
