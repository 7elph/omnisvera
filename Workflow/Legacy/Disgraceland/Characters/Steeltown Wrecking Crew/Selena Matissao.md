## Biography

><h4>“They call me The Phoenix like I’m gonna rise from the shadow in the night. But I don’t hide. I walk in the open, so they remember exactly who’s chasing them.”</h4>


**Selena Matissao** is not a ghost. She’s the firestorm that comes after the ghosts have fled. Once a wife, now a warrior, she carries her pain like a blade — sharp, precise, and always ready to strike.

She lost her husband to one of the Duke’s purges. His death was meant as a warning. Instead, it ignited a legend. Selena rose from the ashes of her old life and carved a new path — one soaked in vengeance and marked by justice. She joined the Wrecking Crew not as a victim, but as a predator.

They call her _The Phoenix_ because she never stops rising. No matter how much blood she spills, no matter how broken the land becomes, Selena burns forward — fierce, focused, and impossible to stop.

She moves through the wilderness like wind through reeds. Her tracking skills are unmatched. Her ability to survive off the grid makes her the Crew’s most valuable scout and assassin. Where others lose the trail, Selena finds it.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Calm, lethal, and calculated — Selena rarely wastes words, and when she acts, it’s with unflinching precision.
**Vices:**  
Obsession with revenge — she pushes herself to the brink chasing ghosts she’s not sure she can ever exorcise.
**Abilities:**  
Master tracker, survivalist, silent killer — she disappears into the wastelands and reemerges with intel, plans… or blood.
**Secrets:**  
Buried beneath a root-tangled tree outside Steeltown is a box holding her wedding ring. She still checks it monthly — not to retrieve it, just to know it’s there.
**Beliefs:**  
Hope must be fought for. Grief must be forged into something sharp. And the land will only heal when its tormentors are gone.
