## Biography

><h4>“You give me a busted carb, a wrench, and five minutes — I’ll get us home. Just don’t ask how.”</h4>

**Joe Ameen** keeps the Crew running — literally. In a world where fuel is rationed and freedom is throttled, Joe’s touch turns rust into revolution. He doesn’t fix machines — he revives them.

Born in the industrial wreckage of Steeltown to immigrants who built the bones of the city, Joe was raised on busted parts and burning dreams. While the Duke hoards fuel and keeps power on a leash, Joe spits in the system’s face with every engine he resurrects.

He grew up alongside [[Mark Jeffrey]] — wrenching, laughing, surviving. Mark brought the heat; Joe brought the spark. Together, they rewired rebellion. Joe became the Crew’s heart, keeping their bikes, trucks, and war rigs howling through the ruins.

But Joe’s more than grease and sarcasm. He believes. In the people. In the movement. In building something that lasts.

And while his hands stay busy under a hood, his mind’s always watching, always listening. When hope runs low, Joe’s laugh is the first thing you hear. When the wheels fall off, he’s already got the spare.

## Personality
**Temperament:**  
Easygoing, dependable, and sharp — Joe’s got the wit to lift spirits and the guts to stay behind when everything explodes.
**Vices:**  
Flirting, fixing, and overworking himself. If it’s broken or beautiful, Joe’s probably halfway in love with it.
**Abilities:**  
Mechanical savant — from bikes to battle rigs, if it runs, Joe can resurrect it. Also a pro at weaponizing junk.
**Secrets:**  
Keeps a broken gold bracelet hidden beneath his tool chest — Katie’s. Found it after a raid. Never gave it back.
**Beliefs:**  
Machines don’t lie. People shouldn’t either. He believes in loyalty, laughter, and legacy — and he builds all three with his bare hands.

## Role In Disgraceland
**Joe Ameen** is the Crew’s soul under the hood. Their engine whisperer. Their fixer of futures. He doesn’t ride point — but without him, the ride doesn’t happen at all.
